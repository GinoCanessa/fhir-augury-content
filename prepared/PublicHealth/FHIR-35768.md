## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive with Modification

**Vote: Persuasive with Modification**

Accept the recommendation to deepen CodeX/mCode alignment and add explicit V2/CDA references, with the following scoped modifications for the next IG version:

1. Add an "Related Specifications" section to the IG introduction that references the production CDA Cancer Registry IG and any applicable V2 messaging specifications.
2. Conduct a systematic review of the current mCode STU (now mature since the original deferral) to identify additional profiles, extensions, or value sets that can be adopted or referenced.
3. Document in the IG where Central Cancer Registry profiles diverge from or extend mCode profiles, with rationale.

**Rationale:** The ticket was deliberately re-opened post-STU1 publication specifically to address this in the next version. The team previously acknowledged the value of deeper mCode review (30–0–0 consensus). mCode has matured significantly since 2022, making a systematic alignment review timely and actionable.

### Disposition B: Considered for Future Use

**Vote: Considered for Future Use**

Defer action again, noting that the current level of CodeX/mCode collaboration remains sufficient for the upcoming ballot cycle and that a comprehensive alignment effort should be planned for a subsequent version when resources allow.

**Rationale:** The original deferral had strong consensus (30–0–0). While the ticket was re-opened, the scope of a full cross-specification alignment is substantial and may again delay publication. The team could focus on higher-priority content changes first.

### Disposition C: Persuasive

**Vote: Persuasive**

Fully accept the recommendation as submitted—reference V2/CDA specs and collaborate with CodeX work—without further scoping or modification.

**Rationale:** The submitter's request is straightforward and the team has already acknowledged its validity. Accepting as-is provides maximum flexibility in how the team implements the collaboration and references.

---

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Persuasive with Modification** is recommended.

The key factors supporting this recommendation:

- **Re-opening was intentional.** Lynn Laakso specifically re-opened this ticket post-STU1 to address it in the next version, signaling that the work group expects action rather than another deferral.
- **Strong prior consensus.** The original 30–0–0 vote acknowledged the recommendation's merit; only timing prevented implementation.
- **mCode maturity.** Since the original 2022 deferral, mCode has progressed through additional STU cycles, making a systematic review more productive now than it would have been previously.
- **Scoping prevents delay.** The "with Modification" framing provides concrete, bounded tasks (add references section, conduct mCode review, document divergences) rather than an open-ended collaboration mandate, reducing the risk of publication delay that motivated the original deferral.
- **CDA basis already established.** The team confirmed the production CDA IG is the basis for this content IG, so explicitly referencing it in the published IG is a low-effort, high-value addition.

## Description

The submitter requests a review of **Group 2** of the concept map — the mapping from the Imaging report header model to the Imaging Diagnostic Report. The submitter notes:

1. They would expect to see substantially the same large amount of information mapped in Group 1 (header model → Imaging Composition) also mapped in Group 2 (header model → Imaging Diagnostic Report).
2. Similar considerations raised about `type` and `formatCode` for Group 1 apply to Group 2 as well.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive (Confirm Resolution)

**Action:** Accept the existing "Persuasive" resolution and confirm that the Group 2 mapping has been updated as part of the most recent mapping revision to include the header model elements that were previously only mapped in Group 1.

**Rationale:** The resolution description states the mapping has already been updated. The linked ticket FHIR-51282 (which was marked "Applied") also addressed ensuring DiagnosticReport holds references to all relevant information, which aligns with this request. Confirming the resolution acknowledges the work done and closes the loop with the submitter.

### Disposition B: Persuasive with Modification — Require Explicit Verification

**Action:** Accept as Persuasive with Modification. Require the editors to verify and document that specific header elements (particularly `type` and `formatCode`) are now correctly mapped in Group 2, and that the Group 2 mapping coverage is consistent with Group 1.

**Rationale:** While the resolution states the mapping was updated, the original concern was specifically about parity between Group 1 and Group 2 mappings and the handling of `type`/`formatCode`. The workgroup may want explicit verification that these specific elements were addressed, not just a general update.

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive — Mapping Groups Serve Different Purposes

**Action:** Mark as Not Persuasive. Explain that Group 1 (header → Composition) and Group 2 (header → DiagnosticReport) serve different structural purposes and are not expected to have identical mapping coverage.

**Rationale:** DiagnosticReport and Composition have different scopes in FHIR. Not all header elements that map to Composition necessarily have equivalent mappings in DiagnosticReport. The current mapping may be intentionally asymmetric.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive (Confirm Resolution)**

The resolution description confirms the mapping has already been updated, and the closely related ticket FHIR-51282 — which specifically addressed ensuring DiagnosticReport holds all relevant references — has already been applied. This indicates the substantive concern has been addressed through the combined work on both tickets. The workgroup should confirm the resolution and verify during review that the updated mapping satisfactorily addresses the submitter's request for Group 2 parity with Group 1.

## Summary

The submitter (Nick Radov) requested that foundational Da Vinci messaging guidelines not be defined within the Alerts IG itself, but instead be placed in a separate, new IG dedicated to messaging fundamentals. The rationale is that the messaging paradigm will be used across multiple future Da Vinci IGs beyond Alerts, and duplicating guidance across IGs should be avoided.

The existing wording under review was a "Note to Balloters" soliciting input on FHIR-related error handling guidance and reliable delivery mechanisms for message-based notifications.

The ticket was resolved as **Persuasive with Modification**: rather than creating a brand-new IG, the resolution directs that future guidance on reliable delivery for message-based notifications will be documented in **base FHIR** and the **HRex IG**, which already addresses this paradigm at [HRex Exchanging via Messaging](http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-hrex/exchanging-messaging.html#message-based-notifications). The related ticket [FHIR-38033](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-38033) further addressed when to move guidance into HRex.

> Note To Balloters — We are actively seeking input on whether additional guidance should be documented in this guide on FHIR-related errors (in addition to normal HTTP errors related to security, header and content type negotiation issues). Additionally, what additional guidance to specify how the Sender can provide a more reliable delivery of notifications to the intended recipient(s). ## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept (Confirm Current Resolution)

**Action:** Confirm the existing "Persuasive with Modification" resolution as-is.

The resolution correctly redirects foundational messaging guidance to two appropriate homes: base FHIR and the Da Vinci HRex IG. The HRex IG already contains content on [exchanging via messaging](http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-hrex/exchanging-messaging.html#message-based-notifications), validating that the commenter's concern about reusability has been addressed. The follow-up ticket FHIR-38033 further codified guidelines for when patterns should migrate into HRex, providing a governance framework. No new separate IG was needed because the existing HRex IG serves the role of cross-cutting Da Vinci foundational guidance.

**Pros:** Aligns with existing work; avoids proliferation of IGs; HRex already fulfills the intent.
**Cons:** None significant — the resolution effectively addressed the original concern through an alternative mechanism.

### Disposition B: Accept with Additional Guidance on Base FHIR Contribution

**Action:** Confirm the resolution, but add a note requesting that the Infrastructure & Messaging work group track whether the promised base FHIR contributions for reliable delivery guidance have been made, and if not, create follow-up trackers to ensure that aspect of the resolution is completed.

The resolution references two venues: base FHIR and HRex. While HRex has documented messaging exchange patterns, it is less clear whether base FHIR itself has received the promised reliable delivery guidance. This disposition ensures both halves of the resolution are fulfilled.

**Pros:** Ensures completeness of the resolution; creates accountability for the base FHIR contribution.
**Cons:** Creates additional tracking overhead; the base FHIR aspect may already be sufficiently covered or no longer relevant given HRex's coverage.

### Disposition C: Decline (Re-open or Reclassify)

**Action:** Re-open the ticket and reconsider whether a dedicated messaging fundamentals IG is warranted given the growth of Da Vinci IGs since 2020.

Since the original submission in 2020, the Da Vinci ecosystem has grown significantly. If multiple Da Vinci IGs now use or plan to use messaging patterns, the original commenter's concern about avoiding repetition may be more pressing than when the ticket was originally resolved. A dedicated messaging fundamentals IG could serve as a normative reference rather than embedding guidance in HRex.

**Pros:** Could provide a cleaner separation of concerns if messaging is widely adopted.
**Cons:** HRex already serves as the foundational Da Vinci IG; creating another IG adds governance and maintenance burden; the resolution was agreed upon through a formal vote process (3-0-1); FHIR-38033 confirmed the current approach is appropriate.

---

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Accept (Confirm Current Resolution)**

The existing resolution adequately addresses the commenter's concern. The original request to create a new messaging fundamentals IG was well-intentioned, but the HRex IG has since become the established home for cross-cutting Da Vinci exchange patterns, including messaging. The resolution explicitly points to HRex's existing messaging content, and the follow-up ticket FHIR-38033 confirmed the governance approach for when patterns should move to HRex. No other Da Vinci IGs have been identified as needing shared messaging fundamentals that aren't already covered by HRex, making a separate IG unnecessary. The resolution should be confirmed without modification.

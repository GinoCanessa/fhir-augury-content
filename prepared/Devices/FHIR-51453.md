## Description

The submitter requests clarification on the use of LOINC and SNOMED CT terminology systems for device observations in the PoCD IG. The ticket quotes existing IG text acknowledging that "LOINC and SNOMED nomenclature systems have concept codes for the majority of the most often used device observations" and proposes defining a slice on `code.coding` with a binding to the MDC Metric Nomenclature to better clarify how SNOMED and LOINC codes should be used alongside MDC codes in device observations.

The device metric and observation profiles already require MDC codes. No further changes are needed.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Considered – No Action Required
**Rationale:** The PoCD IG overview page already addresses the use of LOINC and SNOMED alongside MDC codes in the "Value of the MDC Nomenclature Model" section. The Observation and DeviceMetric profiles intentionally require MDC codes because they define structural mappings from IEEE 11073 to FHIR. Adding a code.coding slice for LOINC/SNOMED would alter the profile's scope beyond its IEEE-mapping purpose. Prior related tickets (FHIR-15373, FHIR-24877) were similarly resolved as not persuasive, establishing a consistent pattern that the PoCD/PHD IGs maintain MDC as the primary required code system. The erikmoll review (2026-03-25) confirmed no changes are needed after checking current content.

### Disposition B: Persuasive with Modification – Add Informative Guidance
**Rationale:** While the existing overview page mentions LOINC/SNOMED availability, the guidance could be strengthened with an explicit informative note encouraging implementers to include LOINC and/or SNOMED CT translations when known, without changing the Observation profile structure or adding required slices. This would address the submitter's intent to "clarify" usage without modifying profile constraints, consistent with erikmoll's initial suggestion (2026-03-09) to add a note to the Overview page. This middle-ground approach improves interoperability guidance while preserving the IEEE-mapping focus.

### Disposition C: Persuasive – Add Optional LOINC/SNOMED Slice
**Rationale:** Define an optional (0..*) slice on Observation.code.coding for LOINC and/or SNOMED CT codes alongside the required MDC coding. This directly addresses the submitter's request and would formalize the already-acknowledged availability of LOINC/SNOMED equivalents. However, this approach was considered and rejected in prior tickets (FHIR-24877 was "Not Persuasive"), and erikmoll specifically recommended against changing the Observation profile since it defines the structural mapping from IEEE 11073 to FHIR.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Considered – No Action Required**

The existing PoCD IG content already addresses the submitter's concern. The overview page explicitly discusses the availability of LOINC and SNOMED equivalents for MDC codes, and the Observation profile intentionally scopes to IEEE 11073 structural mapping. This aligns with the resolution applied by the reviewer (erikmoll), is consistent with the established precedent from multiple prior related tickets (FHIR-15373, FHIR-24877, FHIR-26547), and preserves the IG's focused purpose. Changing the profile or adding terminology slices would conflict with the design intent confirmed repeatedly by the Devices work group.

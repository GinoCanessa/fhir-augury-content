## Summary

This change request asks that the **UDI Domain Analysis Model (DAM)** and the **UDI Pattern Implementation Guide (IG)** be explicitly referenced in the Point of Care Devices (PoCD) IG, and that any future related IG documents also include these references. The ticket targets the "Relationship to Other Projects & Guides" section (or equivalent) of the PoCD IG.

The ticket was originally filed in January 2018 and initially resolved as **Not Persuasive** (vote: Reinhold / Faughn 9-0-0) with a note that the requested content would be added. In 2019, mckenzie adjusted the resolution to "Not Persuasive, but left as Deferred." In March 2026, mcros noted that UDI mapping is already covered in the SDC MDS Device profile mapping but could be addressed more broadly—by including it in the DIM and v2 mappings and mentioning it on the V2, DIM, and SDC mapping pages. In April 2026, a motion to re-open passed **13-0-1** (Erik Moll / Monroe Patillo), and the previous resolution was reverted. The ticket is now **Triaged / Unresolved**.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept — Add UDI DAM and UDI Pattern IG References to PoCD IG

**Resolution:** Persuasive

Accept the request and add explicit references to the UDI DAM and UDI Pattern IG in the PoCD IG's "Relationship to Other Projects & Guides" section. Per mcros's 2026-03-30 comment, also broaden coverage by including UDI references in the DIM and v2 mappings and mentioning them on the V2, DIM, and SDC mapping pages. This aligns with the original acceptance note ("will add the requested content") and the strong reopening vote (13-0-1).

**Rationale:** The workgroup already voted to accept this content in 2018, and the 2026 reopening vote confirms continued interest. UDI support has been added to the Device resource post-STU3 (per jrhoads), making these cross-references timely and accurate. Broadening to DIM/v2/SDC mapping pages as mcros suggested adds value.

### Disposition B: Accept with Narrower Scope — Reference UDI Pattern IG Only

**Resolution:** Persuasive with Modification

Add a reference to the UDI Pattern IG in the PoCD IG but do not reference the UDI DAM, since mcros noted on companion ticket FHIR-15358 that there is "no need to refer to the UDI DAM project under Related Project on home page." Limit the scope to the UDI Pattern IG reference and any future FHIR-specific UDI IGs, excluding the DAM.

**Rationale:** The UDI Pattern IG is directly relevant to FHIR implementers, while the UDI DAM is a higher-level analysis artifact that may not be necessary to call out in an implementation guide. This balances the request with the concern raised by mcros.

### Disposition C: Decline — Not Persuasive

**Resolution:** Not Persuasive

Decline the request. UDI mapping is already covered in the SDC MDS Device profile mapping. Additional cross-references to the UDI DAM and UDI Pattern IG in the PoCD IG would add editorial overhead without providing substantial implementation guidance. Implementers can discover these artifacts through HL7's normal publication channels.

**Rationale:** The PoCD IG's scope is point-of-care device data, and UDI-specific guidance belongs in UDI-focused specifications. Adding cross-references increases maintenance burden across IGs.

---

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Accept — Add UDI DAM and UDI Pattern IG References to PoCD IG**

The workgroup has twice signaled intent to address this: the original 2018 acceptance note stated "will add the requested content," and the 2026 reopening vote passed overwhelmingly (13-0-1). The request is straightforward editorial work—adding references to the "Relationship to Other Projects & Guides" section and broadening UDI coverage in the DIM/v2/SDC mapping pages as mcros recommended. With UDI fields now present in the Device resource, these cross-references help implementers navigate between related specifications. The companion ticket FHIR-15358 should be resolved as a duplicate of this ticket.

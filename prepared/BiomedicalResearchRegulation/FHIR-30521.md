## Details

Daniel Vreeman requests a FHIR representation of CDISC terminology-to-LOINC mappings as companion work to the CDISC Mappings IG. The request spans multiple clinical domains including laboratory (LB), vital signs, and patient-reported outcomes (PROs). The submitter notes that CDISC structural elements could/should align with LOINC term name attributes and other properties, and that Regenstrief's detailed FHIR representation of LOINC makes these properties extractable in computable form.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Considered for Future Use (Retain Current Resolution)

**Rationale:** The request for FHIR-based LOINC-to-CDISC mappings across multiple domains (lab, vitals, PROs) is valuable but represents a significant scope expansion of the CDISC Mappings IG. The existing LOINC-to-LB mapping file on the CDISC website partially addresses the laboratory domain. A FHIR ConceptMap representation of these mappings would require coordination with both CDISC and Regenstrief (LOINC maintainer) and could be pursued in a future ballot cycle when resources and cross-organizational alignment are available.

**Wording:** "Retain current resolution of Considered for Future Use. The work group acknowledges the value of FHIR-based LOINC-to-CDISC terminology mappings but considers this a future enhancement requiring cross-organizational coordination with CDISC and Regenstrief. The existing LOINC-to-LB mapping on the CDISC website partially addresses this need for the laboratory domain."

### Disposition B: Persuasive — Add Informational Reference

**Rationale:** While a full FHIR representation of LOINC-to-CDISC mappings across all domains is out of scope for the current cycle, the IG could add an informational section or reference pointing implementers to the existing CDISC LOINC-to-LB mapping file and describing how such mappings could be represented as FHIR ConceptMap resources. This would provide actionable guidance without requiring the IG to produce and maintain the mappings itself.

**Wording:** "Persuasive with Modification. Add an informational section to the IG that (1) references the existing CDISC LOINC-to-LB mapping file at https://www.cdisc.org/node/7385, (2) describes how LOINC-to-CDISC terminology mappings could be represented as FHIR ConceptMap resources, and (3) identifies vitals, PROs, and other domains as areas for future mapping work."

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive

**Rationale:** The CDISC Mappings IG focuses on structural mappings between FHIR resources and CDISC domains/variables, not on terminology-level mappings between coding systems. LOINC-to-CDISC terminology mappings are maintained by CDISC and Regenstrief as separate artifacts outside the scope of this IG. Adding terminology mappings would change the fundamental nature of the IG and create a maintenance burden for content owned by external organizations.

**Wording:** "Not Persuasive. LOINC-to-CDISC terminology mappings are outside the scope of this IG, which focuses on structural mappings between FHIR resources and CDISC domains. Terminology mappings are maintained by CDISC and Regenstrief as separate artifacts. Implementers should consult the CDISC website for existing LOINC mapping resources."

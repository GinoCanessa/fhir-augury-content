## Summary

The submitter raises ambiguity about what the AI Device profile in the AI Transparency IG is intended to represent — an individual AI algorithm, or a broader AI-based system that may incorporate multiple AI models. The suggestion is that the Device profile should represent the AI-based *system*, while the Model Card profile should represent the individual AI model(s) behind those systems. Additionally, the submitter notes overlapping attributes (manufacturer, version, etc.) between Device and Model Card, arguing that if Device is meant to represent algorithms, a tighter coupling between these two profiles is needed to prevent data inconsistencies. The resolution agrees this needs to be made clearer.

---

## Details

### Description

> Is not entirely clear if the AI Device profile is meant to represent an AI algorithm or a system that utilizes an AI algorithm. As many systems that are being developed are able to utilize an array of AI models, our suggestion is to represent AI-based systems, and the Model Card profile represents the AI model/s behind those systems.
>
> On the other hand, many of the attributes of the Device (manufacturer, version, etc) resource are also present in the model card. So, if the intention is to represent AI algorithms as Device, there should be a tight relation between the attributes of those two objects to mitigate inconsistencies.

### Resolution Description

> Agreed this needs to be made more clear.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept (Persuasive with Modification)

**Action:** Accept the resolution and clarify in the IG that the AI Device profile represents the AI-based *system* (software/device), while the Model Card profile represents the individual AI *model(s)* used by that system. Update the IG introduction, profile descriptions, and general guidance sections to make this distinction explicit. Where attributes overlap between Device and Model Card (manufacturer, version), add guidance specifying which resource is authoritative for each attribute and how they should be kept consistent.

**Rationale:** The submitter's concern is valid and aligns with multiple related tickets (FHIR-54262, FHIR-54264, FHIR-54421) that all identify the same ambiguity. The resolution already agrees this needs clarity. Defining Device as the system-level representation and Model Card as the model-level representation follows the real-world pattern where one AI system may employ multiple AI models, each with its own Model Card. Addressing attribute overlap guidance prevents data quality issues.

### Disposition B: Alternative — Clarify Without Mandating a Specific Hierarchy

**Action:** Accept that the text needs clarification, but rather than mandating that Device always represents the system and Model Card represents the algorithm, add narrative guidance describing both valid use patterns: (1) Device as system + Model Card per model, and (2) Device as individual algorithm + Model Card as its documentation. Provide examples of each pattern and let implementers choose based on their architecture.

**Rationale:** Some implementations may have a 1:1 mapping between Device and algorithm (especially for single-model devices like FDA-cleared radiology AI). Mandating a specific hierarchy may be premature for an early-stage IG. However, this approach risks perpetuating the ambiguity the ticket seeks to resolve and may undermine interoperability.

### Disposition C: Decline (Not Persuasive)

**Action:** Decline the request, keeping the current Device profile definition as-is. The existing text does not strictly preclude either interpretation, and clarification can be deferred to a future ballot cycle once real implementation experience provides stronger guidance.

**Rationale:** The IG is in early ballot stages, and the community may not yet have consensus on whether Device should represent systems or algorithms. However, declining would leave multiple high-priority ballot comments (FHIR-54262, FHIR-54264, FHIR-54421) unaddressed and ignore the resolution already agreed upon.

---

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Accept (Persuasive with Modification)** is recommended. The ambiguity between Device-as-system and Device-as-algorithm is the most frequently raised structural concern in this ballot cycle, with at least five related tickets identifying the same gap. The resolution already acknowledges the need for clarity. Defining Device as the AI system and Model Card as the AI model(s) aligns with real-world patterns where multi-model AI systems are common. This disposition also addresses the attribute overlap concern raised in the ticket, and is consistent with the "Persuasive with Modification" resolution already assigned. The modification should include explicit definitions, attribute guidance, and updated examples throughout the IG.

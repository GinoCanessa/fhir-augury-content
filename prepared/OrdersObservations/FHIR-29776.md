## Summary

The submitter requests a review of the `implantStatus` extension and its associated value set (`http://build.fhir.org/valueset-implantStatus.html`), noting that with the introduction of the DeviceAssociation resource, the implant status concept has been moved into DeviceAssociation but the `.status` element is not currently correct. The ticket suggests the `implantStatus` extension and value set are candidates for deprecation, contingent on how the element is handled within DeviceAssociation. The ticket has been open since November 2020 and remains unresolved, with the most recent comment (August 2025) indicating that further discussion is needed in conjunction with other DeviceAssociation-related tickets.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Deprecate the implantStatus Extension
#### Proposal
Deprecate the `device-implantStatus` extension and its associated value set (`implantStatus`) in the FHIR Extensions Pack. Mark the extension as deprecated with a note directing implementers to use DeviceAssociation resource elements (specifically `DeviceAssociation.associationStatus` and related status elements) to capture implant status information. Retain the extension artifacts for backward compatibility but flag them as not recommended for new implementations.

#### Justification
The DeviceAssociation resource now provides a structured way to represent the relationship between a device and a patient, including association status. Comment from lconstab confirms that "DeviceAssociation now covers this." The extension was created before DeviceAssociation existed and represents functionality that has been subsumed by the resource. Deprecation aligns with the original submitter's suggestion and reduces redundancy in the specification. The related tickets FHIR-54971, FHIR-54463, and FHIR-55254 are actively addressing DeviceAssociation's status elements, code systems, and value sets, which further supports the transition away from the extension.

---

### Disposition B: Defer Until DeviceAssociation Status Elements Are Finalized
#### Proposal
Defer action on this ticket until the DeviceAssociation status-related tickets (FHIR-54971, FHIR-54463, FHIR-55254) are resolved. Once the DeviceAssociation resource's `status`, `associationStatus`, and `statusReason` elements are finalized and their value sets are settled, revisit this ticket to determine whether the `implantStatus` extension can be fully deprecated or whether it captures concepts not adequately addressed by DeviceAssociation.

#### Justification
As noted by hbuitendijk in 2021, implant status and association status may not fully overlap — a device can be implanted but not functional, which is a distinct concept from association status. The most recent comment from marti_velezis (August 2025) explicitly states that this "needs additional discussion with the other DeviceAssociation tickets." FHIR-54971 is actively working on distinguishing `status`, `associationStatus`, and a potential `availabilityStatus` in DeviceAssociation. Until those elements are finalized, deprecating the extension prematurely could leave a gap for representing implant-specific functional status. Deferring ensures a complete and coordinated resolution.

---

### Disposition C: Decline
#### Proposal
Decline the request and retain the `implantStatus` extension as-is. The extension captures a concept (the functional status of an implanted device) that is distinct from DeviceAssociation's association status and should continue to be available in the Extensions Pack.

#### Justification
The `implantStatus` extension addresses a specific use case — whether an implanted device is functional, non-functional, or disabled — that may not be fully captured by DeviceAssociation's `associationStatus` element, which focuses on the relationship between the device and the patient (implanted, explanted, etc.). Retaining the extension preserves backward compatibility and avoids forcing implementers to migrate to an approach that may not fully cover their needs. However, this disposition risks maintaining redundant artifacts if DeviceAssociation ultimately addresses all relevant concepts.

---

### Recommendation
**Recommended disposition:** B (Defer Until DeviceAssociation Status Elements Are Finalized)

The strongest path forward is to defer this ticket until the active DeviceAssociation work is complete. The most recent comment from marti_velezis explicitly calls for coordination with other DeviceAssociation tickets, and three high-priority tickets (FHIR-54971, FHIR-54463, FHIR-55254) are actively addressing the DeviceAssociation status model, value set quality, and THO migration. The 2021 comment from hbuitendijk raises a valid concern that implant functional status and association status may be distinct concepts, and this nuance should be resolved as part of the DeviceAssociation work before deprecation proceeds. Once those tickets are resolved, the workgroup will have the clarity needed to make a definitive decision on whether the `implantStatus` extension should be deprecated, merged into DeviceAssociation concepts, or retained. Deferring prevents premature deprecation while ensuring the topic is not forgotten.

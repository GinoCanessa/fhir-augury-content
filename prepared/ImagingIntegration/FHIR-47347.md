## Description

The table listing DICOM containers in the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG is missing a row for the **Imaging Measurement Group CONTAINER**. The reporter (Ana Kostadinovska) identified that the container hierarchy diagram was incomplete and did not accurately reflect the structure defined in DICOM TID 1500 (Measurement Report).

## Resolution Description

The resolution proposes updating the diagram to add the missing container. An updated diagram was provided as part of the resolution.

## Discussion History

The ticket has had active discussion between **Ana Kostadinovska** (reporter) and **Jonathan Whitby** (editor/implementer):

1. **2025-11-06** — Ana noted the initial proposed diagram was still incorrect and drafted a replacement diagram showing the proper TID 1500 container hierarchy (attached as `DICOMSRTID1500.png`).
2. **2025-11-07** — Jonathan acknowledged the error and updated the proposed resolution. He also identified that the **Qualitative Evaluations Container** was at the same level in TID 1500 (not nested as shown).
3. **2025-12-05** — Ana raised further concerns: she could not see that Numerical Measurement and Qualitative Evaluation are contained within the Measurements Group per the DICOM standard (TID 1500, Part 16, Chapter A). She also questioned whether Derived Imaging Measurements Container contains a nested Measurements Group.
4. **2026-01-06** — Jonathan identified a remaining fix: the Qualitative Evaluations Container should contain 0..* QE directly with no middle container.
5. **2026-01-16** — Jonathan confirmed the diagram was updated as discussed. He also fixed a similar issue on the [mapping overview page](https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/dicom-sr/en/mapping.html#dicom-sr-measurement-report-mapping-overview).

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive with Modification (as proposed)

**Action:** Accept the existing resolution — update the container hierarchy diagram to add the missing Imaging Measurement Group CONTAINER and correct the nesting structure per TID 1500.

**Rationale:** The diagram update has already been implemented by Jonathan Whitby through iterative review with the reporter. The corrections align the IG's container table and diagram with the authoritative DICOM TID 1500 specification (Part 16, Chapter A, Section TID 1500). Specific fixes include:
- Adding the missing Imaging Measurement Group CONTAINER row
- Correcting the Qualitative Evaluations Container to contain 0..* QE directly (no middle container)
- Fixing a similar issue on the mapping overview page

This is the most straightforward path since the work is largely complete.

### Disposition B: Persuasive — Apply with broader table audit

**Action:** Accept as Persuasive and additionally perform a comprehensive audit of all container tables and diagrams in the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG against the current DICOM TID 1500 specification.

**Rationale:** The iterative comment history (five rounds of corrections between Nov 2025 and Jan 2026) suggests the container hierarchy is complex and error-prone. A broader audit would catch any remaining discrepancies beyond the specific items addressed in this ticket. This reduces the risk of future tickets for similar issues but requires more effort.

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive — Request formal verification

**Action:** Defer until a formal side-by-side comparison of the IG's container hierarchy against DICOM TID 1500 is provided as part of the resolution.

**Rationale:** The extended back-and-forth in comments suggests the diagram corrections were made incrementally and may still contain errors. A formal verification document would provide confidence in the accuracy. However, this would delay an otherwise reasonable fix and places additional burden on the editor.

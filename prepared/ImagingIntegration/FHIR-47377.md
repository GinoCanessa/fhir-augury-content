## Description

The reporter identifies a misalignment between two diagrams representing the **Imaging Measurements Container** in the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG. Specifically, the diagram in one section of the IG includes **Evaluations** and **Derived Measurements** as part of the Imaging Measurements Container, while the diagram in section 3.1.4.2 does **not** include them within the container. The reporter requests that the diagrams be aligned to present a consistent model.

## Analysis

This is a **documentation/diagram consistency** issue within the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG. The core problem is that two diagrams in the IG present conflicting views of the Imaging Measurements Container's scope — one includes Evaluations and Derived Measurements inside the container, while the other (section 3.1.4.2) excludes them. This creates confusion for implementers about the correct structural model.

**FHIR-47384** is a near-duplicate of this ticket, filed by the same reporter on the same day, raising the same type of concern but referencing section 4.1 instead of section 3.1.4.2. The workgroup should consider resolving these two tickets together.

The fix requires the IG editors to determine the **authoritative model** for the Imaging Measurements Container (whether Evaluations and Derived Measurements are contained within it or are siblings) and then update all diagrams throughout the IG to match.

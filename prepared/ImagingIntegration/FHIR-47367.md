## Description

The submitter observes that the DICOM SR to FHIR mapping IG currently states:

> "If an Imaging Measurement Group, Imaging Measurement or Imaging Qualitative Evaluation has a child with Concept Name DCM#111001 'Algorithm Name' then it is mapped to an Algorithm Identification Device."

The **Derived Imaging Measurement** profile is omitted from this list. The submitter argues that derived observations (calculations, scores, etc.) may also be generated by specific algorithms, and therefore the Derived Imaging Measurement profile should also be eligible for mapping to an Algorithm Identification Device.

## Resolution Description

The proposed resolution (marked **Persuasive**) is to update **both** lists in the relevant section to include the Derived Imaging Measurement profile:

> If an [Imaging Measurement Group](StructureDefinition-imaging-measurement-group.html), [Imaging Measurement](StructureDefinition-imaging-measurement.html), [Imaging Qualitative Evaluation](StructureDefinition-imaging-qualitative-evaluation.html) or [Derived Imaging Measurement](StructureDefinition-derived-imaging-measurement.html)...

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive (Recommended)

**Action:** Accept the change as proposed — add "Derived Imaging Measurement" to both lists in the Algorithm Identification Device mapping section of the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG.

**Rationale:** The omission appears to be an oversight. Derived Imaging Measurements (e.g., calculated scores, derived values) can be produced by named algorithms just as direct measurements can. There is no technical or conceptual reason to exclude them from Algorithm Identification Device mapping. The fix is minimal (adding one profile reference to two existing lists), does not introduce breaking changes, and is consistent with how the other observation profiles in the IG are handled. The reporter and commenter both agree the change is appropriate.

### Disposition B: Accept as Persuasive with Modification

**Action:** Accept the intent but also verify that the Derived Imaging Measurement profile's `device` element cardinality and type constraints are compatible with referencing an Algorithm Identification Device, and update constraints if needed.

**Rationale:** While adding Derived Imaging Measurement to the text is straightforward, the workgroup may want to confirm that the StructureDefinition for Derived Imaging Measurement already supports a Device reference in the `device` element with the same constraints as Imaging Measurement and Imaging Qualitative Evaluation. If there is a discrepancy, additional profile updates may be warranted alongside the text change.

### Disposition C: Reject as Not Persuasive

**Action:** Leave the mapping text unchanged, excluding Derived Imaging Measurement from Algorithm Identification Device mapping.

**Rationale:** One could argue that derived measurements are inherently the product of computation and that their algorithmic provenance is already implicit, making explicit Algorithm Identification Device mapping unnecessary or redundant. However, this argument is weak — the other profiles' algorithm mappings serve documentation and traceability purposes, and excluding derived measurements creates an inconsistency.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive.** The change is a clear gap fix — Derived Imaging Measurements can be algorithm-produced just like direct measurements and qualitative evaluations, and should be eligible for the same Algorithm Identification Device mapping. The proposed resolution text is minimal, non-breaking, and already agreed upon by the reporter.

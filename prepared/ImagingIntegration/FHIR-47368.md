## Description

The reporter identifies an unclear sentence in the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG:

> "All algorithm devices will populate use the Equipment Device to populate the parent element."

The sentence contains a grammatical error — the phrase "will populate use" appears to be a conflation of two constructions, resulting in a redundant "populate." The reporter requests clarification.

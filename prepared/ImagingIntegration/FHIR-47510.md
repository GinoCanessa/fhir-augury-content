## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive (Rename the Section)

**Action:** Rename the section title to **"Imaging Measurement Group Mapping"** (or a similar FHIR-oriented title) to align with the naming convention used in all other example subsections.

**Rationale:** The IG consistently uses FHIR-oriented terminology in section titles throughout the examples. A single section using DICOM-specific naming breaks this pattern and creates confusion for readers about whether the section describes DICOM or FHIR concepts. This is a low-risk editorial change that improves consistency and readability. The closely related ticket FHIR-47489 (adding "resource" to subsection names) reflects the same goal of clarifying FHIR vs. DICOM terminology, and both changes could be applied together.

**Impact:** Editorial only — section title change. No structural or normative impact.

### Disposition B: Persuasive with Modification (Broader Title Consistency Pass)

**Action:** Accept the rename, but also conduct a comprehensive review of all section titles in the Examples section to ensure consistent FHIR-oriented naming throughout, addressing this ticket alongside FHIR-47489 and FHIR-47655.

**Rationale:** Multiple tickets from the same ballot cycle (FHIR-47489, FHIR-47655, FHIR-47223) point to naming and structural inconsistencies in the examples and mapping sections. Resolving them together in a single consistency pass would be more efficient and produce a better result than piecemeal fixes.

**Impact:** Slightly broader editorial scope; still non-normative. May affect multiple section headings and cross-references within the IG.

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive (Keep Current Title)

**Action:** Retain the existing section title.

**Rationale:** The current title may intentionally use DICOM terminology to indicate the source concept being mapped. If the IG's pattern is "source → target," using the DICOM name in the title could be considered appropriate since the section describes a mapping *from* a DICOM SR structure. Changing to a FHIR-oriented title might obscure what is being mapped.

**Impact:** No change required.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Persuasive** — The request is straightforward, well-justified, and low-risk. The reporter correctly identifies that the section title is inconsistent with the naming pattern used throughout the rest of the IG's examples. Renaming to "Imaging Measurement Group Mapping" (or equivalent FHIR-oriented title) improves consistency and readability without affecting normative content. The work group may also wish to coordinate with FHIR-47489 for a combined title consistency fix, but this ticket stands well on its own.

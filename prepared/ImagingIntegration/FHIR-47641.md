## Description

The profiles defined in the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG are missing descriptive text. The reporter suggests adding descriptions for all profiles explaining their purpose and when they should be used.

## Keywords

`DICOM SR`, `profile description`, `BodyStructure`, `Device`, `finding site`, `general equipment`, `tracking identifier`, `documentation`, `IG artifacts`

## Resolution Summary

The resolution proposes updated descriptions for the IG profiles. Two examples provided so far (work in progress):

1. **DICOM SR Finding Site** (`dicom-sr-finding-site`): *"DICOM® SR Finding Site Mapping to BodyStructure. Used to represent the finding site and tracking identifiers associated with a measurement or qualitative evaluation."* (Note: other tickets propose merging this profile with the tracking identifier profile.)
2. **DICOM General Equipment** (`dicom-general-equipment`): *"DICOM® General Equipment to Device Mapping. Used to represent the equipment that produced the measurements or qualitative evaluations reported in a DICOM® SR document."*

The resolution is marked as in progress — not all profile descriptions have been drafted yet.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive (Accept as-is)

Accept the resolution as Persuasive. The proposed descriptions for `dicom-sr-finding-site` and `dicom-general-equipment` are a meaningful improvement. Complete the remaining profile descriptions following the same pattern (purpose + when to use). Address the reporter's feedback by adding clarification on when DICOM Participant maps to Device vs. Practitioner.

**Rationale:** The ticket identifies a real documentation gap. The in-progress resolution demonstrates an appropriate approach — concise descriptions that explain each profile's purpose and mapping context. Completing the work fulfills the ballot comment.

### Disposition B: Persuasive with Modification

Accept as Persuasive with a modification requiring that **all** profile descriptions be completed before the ticket is closed, and that the reporter's specific feedback (Device vs. Practitioner mapping guidance for DICOM Participant) be explicitly addressed in the relevant profile description(s). Also coordinate with FHIR-47388 (BodyStructure profile consolidation) and FHIR-50895 (finding site / tracking identifier merge) to ensure descriptions remain consistent after any profile restructuring.

**Rationale:** The resolution is partially complete — only two of many profiles have proposed descriptions. Accepting without requiring completion risks leaving the documentation gap partially unaddressed. Cross-referencing with related profile restructuring tickets ensures descriptions are not outdated on arrival.

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive

Reject the change request. Profile descriptions, while helpful, are supplementary documentation that can be added incrementally without a formal ballot resolution. The IG build page already lists all profiles, and implementers can refer to the StructureDefinition narratives directly.

**Rationale:** This would be appropriate only if the WG determines that profile descriptions are out of scope for ballot resolution or that the current IG artifacts page provides sufficient context. However, this is a weak position given the multiple related tickets also requesting improved documentation.

## Recommendation

**Disposition B: Persuasive with Modification**

The ticket correctly identifies that profile descriptions are missing and the proposed resolution is headed in the right direction, but the work is explicitly incomplete ("in progress"). The reporter's most recent feedback (Oct 2025) confirms improvement but highlights a remaining gap around Device/Practitioner mapping guidance. Accepting with modification ensures: (1) all profiles receive descriptions, not just two, (2) the specific Device vs. Practitioner clarification is addressed, and (3) the descriptions are coordinated with related profile restructuring work in FHIR-47388 and FHIR-50895 to avoid inconsistency. This disposition respects the ballot commenter's intent while setting a clear completion bar.

## Description

The submitter requests that the PACIO Functional Status IG include a future roadmap outlining upcoming scope expansions. The PACIO project is making incremental progress towards interoperability of post-acute care content, but the present IG's scope is unclear. A roadmap would help readers understand what is not covered by the current guide and what is planned. Suggested roadmap items include Instrumental ADLs, communication, sensory impairments, and other future efforts.

## Prior Disposition History

This ticket was previously resolved as **Considered for Future Use** on 2021-06-28 with a vote of Caleb Wan / Jay Lyle: **6-0-0**. The rationale noted that an effort to add run-time filtered value sets to FHIR was underway, which would likely reduce the need for separate cognitive status, functional status, and potentially future PAC-related profiles, and would provide the future direction for the PACIO work.

The ticket was subsequently **re-opened** by lynn_laakso on 2021-11-04 for consideration post STU1 publication.

## Analysis

### Context
This ticket is part of a cluster of high-priority feedback items filed by Daniel Vreeman during the STU1 ballot of the PACIO Functional Status IG (December 2020). The feedback collectively addresses: (1) unclear scope, (2) the case for merging the Functional Status and Cognitive Status IGs, and (3) the need for a roadmap. All three were initially resolved as "Considered for Future Use" with unanimous 6-0-0 votes in mid-2021, then re-opened in November 2021 for post-STU1 consideration.

### Key Considerations
- **PACIO evolution**: Since these tickets were filed, the PACIO project has evolved significantly. The run-time filtered value set effort mentioned in the 2021 disposition was expected to reduce the need for separate IGs — the current state of that effort should inform disposition.
- **Sibling ticket**: FHIR-29979 is the identical request filed against the Cognitive Status IG. These should be resolved consistently.
- **Scope cluster**: FHIR-30014 (merge IGs) and FHIR-30015 (clarify scope) are tightly coupled to this roadmap request. A roadmap is most useful after scope and IG structure decisions are made.
- **Stale since 2021**: No activity on this ticket since November 2021, suggesting it may have been overtaken by the PACIO Personal Functioning and Engagement (PFE) IG evolution.

---

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive with Modification
**Action:** Accept the request but scope it to adding a brief "Future Directions" section to the IG rather than a full formal roadmap document.

**Rationale:** A concise future directions section is practical, sets expectations for implementers, and is consistent with how other IGs communicate planned evolution. The full roadmap concept (with timelines and detailed scope) is impractical to maintain in a normative publication. This approach addresses the submitter's core concern — that readers cannot tell what is coming next — without creating a maintenance burden.

**Specific changes:** Add a "Future Directions" section to the IG home page or background page listing planned areas of expansion (e.g., Instrumental ADLs, communication, sensory impairments, alignment with ICF framework, potential IG consolidation).

### Disposition B: Not Persuasive with Modification
**Action:** Decline the roadmap request but acknowledge the underlying concern by improving the scope narrative.

**Rationale:** A roadmap in a ballot-published IG quickly becomes stale and potentially misleading. The PACIO project has its own project management channels for communicating plans. The better solution is the scope clarification already requested in FHIR-30015; if that ticket is addressed properly, the need for a separate roadmap diminishes. The IG should focus on what it covers, not what it might cover in the future.

**Specific changes:** Close this ticket and ensure FHIR-30015 (scope clarification) addresses the implicit need for context about what is and isn't in scope.

### Disposition C: Considered for Future Use
**Action:** Defer again pending resolution of the IG consolidation question (FHIR-30014) and the current state of the PACIO PFE IG.

**Rationale:** The PACIO project has been evolving toward a unified Personal Functioning and Engagement (PFE) IG. A roadmap for the standalone Functional Status IG may be moot if the IGs are merged. It is more efficient to revisit this request once the structural direction is decided.

**Specific changes:** None to the IG now; revisit after FHIR-30014 (merge IGs) is resolved.

---

## Recommendation

**Disposition C: Considered for Future Use** is recommended.

The roadmap request is reasonable in principle, but premature to act on while the fundamental IG structure question (FHIR-30014, merge Functional Status and Cognitive Status) remains unresolved. The sibling ticket FHIR-29979 (same request on the Cognitive Status IG) reinforces that these two IGs share a common trajectory. The PACIO project's evolution toward the Personal Functioning and Engagement (PFE) IG may render a standalone Functional Status roadmap obsolete. Once the consolidation direction is settled, a "Future Directions" section (per Disposition A) could be added to whichever IG structure emerges. This disposition is also consistent with the prior 6-0-0 vote from June 2021.

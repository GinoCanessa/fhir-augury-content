## Summary

Daniel Vreeman requests that the PACIO Cognitive Status IG narrative be substantially expanded to clarify the IG's scope. Three specific concerns are raised:

1. **Domain definition**: The IG does not define "cognitive status" or identify what kinds of information it covers. The reporter recommends anchoring the IG to the WHO's International Classification of Functioning, Disability and Health (ICF) framework, which aligns with future ICD-11 and provides a globally recognized structure for functioning and disability domains.

2. **Terminology ("status" vs. "functioning")**: The term "status" implies a singular axis of assessment, but the ICF and CMS-required assessment instruments describe multiple dimensions of functioning (current performance, capability, goals, etc.) influenced by physiological, personal, and environmental factors. The reporter proposes using the umbrella term "functioning" with ICF-based subdomain labels (e.g., "mental functioning"), consistent with USCDI proposals. This is linked to the companion proposal to merge the Cognitive Status and Functional Status IGs (FHIR-29977).

3. **Resource type scope**: The IG focuses heavily on Observations from CMS-required assessment instruments. It is unclear whether Conditions, Goals, Medications, Interventions, or other resource types are in scope now or in the future.

## Contextual Notes

- **Post-ballot evolution**: The PACIO project has since merged the Cognitive Status and Functional Status IGs into the **US PACIO Personal Functioning and Engagement (PFE)** IG, as evidenced by FHIR-38054 being filed against that successor specification. This directly addresses part of the reporter's recommendation.
- **ICF adoption confirmed**: Comments from lynn_laakso confirm PACIO adopted the ICF conceptual framework and broadened the scope to include mental functioning observations — partially addressing this ticket.
- **Naming issue deferred**: The suggestion to replace "status" with "functioning" was explicitly deferred to be resolved alongside the IG merge (FHIR-29977). The successor IG title "Personal Functioning and Engagement" reflects adoption of this feedback.
- **Previous resolution reverted**: Originally resolved as "Considered for Future Use" (6-0-0 vote, 2021-06-28), then re-opened by lynn_laakso on 2021-11-04 for post-STU1 consideration. Currently Triaged with no assignee.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive (Resolved by Successor IG)
**Rationale**: The core requests in this ticket have been substantially addressed through the evolution of the PACIO project. The Cognitive Status and Functional Status IGs were merged into the US PACIO Personal Functioning and Engagement (PFE) IG, which: (1) adopts the ICF framework as recommended, (2) uses "functioning" rather than "status" in its title, and (3) defines broader scope including mental functioning domains. Resolve as Persuasive, noting that the successor PFE IG incorporates the requested changes. Any remaining gaps in ICF mapping detail are tracked separately under FHIR-38054.

### Disposition B: Persuasive with Modification
**Rationale**: Acknowledge the feedback as valid and note that substantial progress has been made (ICF adoption, IG merger, broader scope), but resolve with a modification stating that the specific request for detailed resource type scope clarification (Conditions, Goals, Medications, Interventions) should be tracked as a new ticket against the PFE IG if not yet addressed in its current narrative. This ensures the third concern (resource type coverage) is not lost.

### Disposition C: Considered for Future Use
**Rationale**: While significant progress has been made, some aspects of the original request may not yet be fully addressed in the current PFE IG narrative — particularly the detailed scope clarification around which resource types (beyond Observation) are covered and the depth of ICF subdomain mapping. Defer for future consideration in a subsequent PFE ballot cycle, allowing the workgroup to evaluate the current state of the PFE narrative against each point raised.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Persuasive (Resolved by Successor IG)** is recommended. The three core concerns — ICF framework adoption, replacing "status" with broader "functioning" terminology, and merging the IGs — have all been addressed by the creation of the US PACIO Personal Functioning and Engagement IG. The residual concern about ICF mapping depth is already tracked under FHIR-38054 (resolved as Persuasive). Closing this ticket as Persuasive acknowledges the validity of the feedback and documents that it has been acted upon, while avoiding duplication with active PFE-era tickets.

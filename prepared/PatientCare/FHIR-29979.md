## Description

The submitter (Daniel Vreeman) requests that the PACIO Cognitive Status IG include a future roadmap section outlining "what's coming" to help readers understand what is not covered by the current guide. The PACIO project is making incremental progress toward interoperability of important post-acute care content, and the present scope of the IG is unclear. The suggested roadmap could cover planned future areas such as Instrumental ADLs, communication, sensory impairments, or other future efforts.

## Analysis

### Context

This ticket was submitted during the STU1 ballot cycle for the PACIO Cognitive Status IG (December 2020) as part of a trio of related feedback items from Daniel Vreeman (FHIR-29977, FHIR-29978, FHIR-29979). The core theme across all three is that the IG's scope and future direction are unclear. This particular ticket specifically requests a roadmap section in the IG.

The ticket was initially resolved as "Considered for Future Use" (voted 6-0-0 on 2021-06-28), but was subsequently re-opened by lynn_laakso on 2021-11-04 for consideration post-STU1 publication. The comment at the time of the original resolution noted that efforts to add run-time filtered value sets to FHIR could reduce the need for separate cognitive/functional status profiles and would likely provide future direction for PACIO.

Since then, the PACIO project has in fact evolved — the Personal Functioning and Engagement (PFE) IG has emerged as a successor that merges and broadens the cognitive and functional status IGs, partially addressing the concerns in sibling ticket FHIR-29977. This evolution is relevant context for whether a roadmap in the original Cognitive Status IG is still needed or whether the PFE IG itself serves as the "roadmap" outcome.

### Key Considerations

- The ticket has **Highest priority** and the **Fit-For-Teleconference** label, indicating it was flagged for workgroup discussion.
- The original resolution (Considered for Future Use) was reverted — the workgroup needs to re-evaluate.
- The sibling tickets (FHIR-29977, FHIR-29978) are also Triaged/Unresolved and address scope clarity and IG merging — all three should ideally be considered together.
- The PACIO project has evolved significantly since 2020; the PFE IG may address the roadmap request implicitly.
- No active community discussion about a roadmap section in the Cognitive Status IG was found on Zulip or GitHub.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive — Add Roadmap Section to IG
**Action:** Add a new narrative section to the PACIO Cognitive Status IG that outlines planned future directions, including Instrumental ADLs, communication, sensory impairments, and the relationship to the broader PFE IG.
**Rationale:** The submitter's request is reasonable and addresses a real gap — readers and implementers benefit from understanding what's in scope now vs. what's planned. A roadmap would reduce confusion and set expectations. The 6-0-0 original vote acknowledged the merit. Since the IG is still published and referenced, adding a roadmap section is a low-effort, high-value change.
**Trade-offs:** If the Cognitive Status IG is being superseded by the PFE IG, maintaining a roadmap in a soon-to-be-deprecated IG may create confusion or become stale.

### Disposition B: Not Persuasive — Resolved by Evolution to PFE IG
**Action:** Close as Not Persuasive with comment explaining that the PACIO project has evolved and the PFE IG now serves as the broader framework that the submitter was requesting. Recommend the submitter review the PFE IG.
**Rationale:** The underlying concern (unclear future direction) has been addressed by the project's natural evolution. The PFE IG effectively *is* the roadmap — it merges cognitive and functional status, broadens the scope, and anchors to a functioning framework. Adding a roadmap to the now-superseded Cognitive Status IG is unnecessary.
**Trade-offs:** If the Cognitive Status IG remains independently published and used, readers still lack context about how it fits into the broader PACIO ecosystem.

### Disposition C: Considered for Future Use — Defer to Next Ballot Cycle
**Action:** Re-apply the original "Considered for Future Use" resolution, deferring to the next ballot cycle when the relationship between the Cognitive Status IG and the PFE IG is clearer.
**Rationale:** The PACIO project is in transition. It is premature to add a roadmap to the Cognitive Status IG until the workgroup has decided whether this IG will be maintained alongside PFE or deprecated in its favor. This avoids creating content that may immediately become obsolete.
**Trade-offs:** The ticket has been open since 2020 and was already deferred once; further deferral may frustrate submitters and leaves the IG without context about its future.

## Recommendation

**Disposition B: Not Persuasive — Resolved by Evolution to PFE IG**

The strongest path forward is to acknowledge that the PACIO project's evolution to the PFE IG has substantively addressed the submitter's concern about unclear future direction. The PFE IG merges cognitive and functional status content and provides the broader framework that was requested. Rather than adding a roadmap section to an IG that is being superseded, the workgroup should close this ticket with a clear explanation pointing implementers to the PFE IG. This should be considered in conjunction with sibling tickets FHIR-29977 (merge IGs) and FHIR-29978 (clarify scope), which address the same underlying concern and may also be resolved by the PFE evolution. If the workgroup determines that the Cognitive Status IG will continue to be independently maintained, then Disposition A should be reconsidered.

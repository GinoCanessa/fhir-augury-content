## Description

The submitter requests adding a "Support" menu to the red menu bar of the CARIN Digital Insurance Card IG. The Support menu would provide implementers and community members with convenient access to key HL7 resources. The proposed sub-menu items are:

1. **Discussion Forum** — Link to the [CARIN IG for Digital Insurance Card Zulip channel](https://chat.fhir.org/#narrow/channel/285193-CARIN-IG-for-Digital-Insurance-Card/topic/Implementations.20and.20Examples/with/507783712)
2. **Project Page** — Link to the [CARIN IG for Digital Insurance Card Confluence page](https://confluence.hl7.org/spaces/CAR/pages/104579206/CARIN+IG+for+Digital+Insurance+Card)
3. **Implementer Support Page** — Link to the [Implementer Support - STU2 Confluence page](https://confluence.hl7.org/spaces/CAR/pages/391187088/Implementer+Support+-STU2)
4. **Specification Dashboard** — Link to the [Jira Dashboard](https://jira.hl7.org/secure/Dashboard.jspa?selectPageId=14305)
5. **Propose a Change** — Link to [create a Jira ticket](https://jira.hl7.org/secure/CreateIssueDetails!init.jspa?pid=10405&issuetype=10600&customfield_11302=FHIR-us-carin-dic) pre-populated for the DIC specification

## Analysis

This is a straightforward IG navigation enhancement that follows an established pattern already applied to other CARIN and Da Vinci IGs (FHIR-46269 for PCT, FHIR-46270 for Blue Button). The submitter provides all six specific links for the sub-menu items. The resolution statement confirms the change was already implemented and reviewed at the CARIN DIC Community meeting on 2025-10-03. The resolution is marked **Persuasive**, meaning it was accepted as submitted.

This is a low-risk, high-usability change that improves discoverability of community resources for implementers.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive (Apply as Submitted)
Accept the ticket as resolved. The Support menu with all six sub-menu items (Discussion Forum, Project Page, Implementer Support, Specification Dashboard, Propose a Change, Downloads) should be added to the IG's red menu bar exactly as specified, with the provided URLs. This aligns with the established pattern from FHIR-46269 and FHIR-46270 and was already reviewed by the community.

### Disposition B: Accept as Persuasive with Modification
Accept the change but verify that all provided URLs are current and functional before publication. Specifically, confirm the Implementer Support page (STU2-specific link) and the Jira Dashboard link resolve correctly, as these may change between ballot cycles. Consider standardizing the menu item names to match conventions used in FHIR-46269/46270 for cross-IG consistency.

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive
Defer the change on the grounds that IG menu bar structure should be standardized across all HL7 IGs by the IG publisher tooling or templates, rather than managed per-IG via individual Jira tickets. However, this would delay a useful improvement that is already community-reviewed and follows existing precedent.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive (Apply as Submitted)**

The change was already implemented and reviewed at the CARIN DIC Community meeting on 2025-10-03. It follows a well-established pattern already published in two other IGs (FHIR-46269, FHIR-46270). All six URLs are explicitly provided and the scope is clearly defined. There is no technical risk or normative impact — this is purely an IG navigation improvement. Accepting as-is is the most efficient path forward.

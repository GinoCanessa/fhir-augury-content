## Summary

Joan Harper requests that ValueSet canonical URLs in the CARIN Digital Insurance Card (DIC) IG be anchored in the HL7 Terminology (THO) at `terminology.hl7.org` rather than at `hl7.org/fhir`. A UTG ticket should be created to register the ValueSet in THO. The ticket notes that only ValueSets with a required binding to a `code` data type should remain anchored at `hl7.fhir.org`.

This ticket was originally resolved as "Considered for Future Use" in March 2022 (vote: 11-0-1, Mark Roberts/Cille Kissel), with the rationale that the workgroup acknowledged the need but would defer until the Vocabulary WG formalized the THO registration process. That resolution was reverted by Lynn Laakso in July 2022 to re-open issues after STU publication. The current resolution is **Persuasive**.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept (Persuasive)

**Action:** Accept as Persuasive. Migrate the ValueSet canonical URL from `hl7.org/fhir` to `terminology.hl7.org` (THO) and create a UTG ticket to formally register the ValueSet in THO. Only ValueSets with a required binding to a `code` data type should retain their `hl7.fhir.org` anchor.

**Rationale:** This aligns with HL7's terminology governance strategy of centralizing ValueSet definitions in THO. The workgroup previously acknowledged the validity of this request (11-0-1 vote) and the current resolution is already Persuasive. The Vocabulary WG has had time since the original deferral to formalize the THO registration process. Implementing this change improves consistency across HL7 IGs and ensures proper terminology governance. This should be coordinated with the batch of sibling tickets (FHIR-35552 through FHIR-35555, etc.) for a unified approach.

### Disposition B: Accept with Deferral (Persuasive with Modification)

**Action:** Accept as Persuasive with Modification. Acknowledge the request and plan the migration for a future ballot cycle, contingent on confirmation from the Vocabulary WG that the THO registration process is ready. Create a tracking item to coordinate all related sibling tickets together.

**Rationale:** While the request is valid, the original deferral cited the need for a formalized process from the Vocabulary WG. If that process is not yet fully in place, a coordinated batch migration in a future cycle would reduce risk and ensure all related ValueSets are handled consistently. This avoids piecemeal changes.

### Disposition C: Decline (Not Persuasive)

**Action:** Decline as Not Persuasive. Retain the current `hl7.org/fhir`-anchored canonical URLs for ValueSets in the CARIN DIC IG.

**Rationale:** Changing canonical URLs is a breaking change for implementers who have already adopted the CARIN DIC IG. The current URLs are functional and resolvable. Migrating to THO introduces implementation burden without immediate clinical benefit. The workgroup could argue that stability for existing implementers outweighs the governance alignment benefit, particularly given that many IGs still use `hl7.org/fhir`-anchored URLs.

---

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Accept (Persuasive)** is recommended. The request is well-founded and consistent with HL7's direction for terminology governance. The workgroup has already voted in favor (11-0-1) and the resolution is Persuasive. The THO migration aligns with broader HL7 policy, and the sibling tickets indicate this is a systematic issue that should be addressed. Coordinating with the Vocabulary WG to execute the migration across all affected ValueSets in a single effort will ensure consistency and minimize disruption.

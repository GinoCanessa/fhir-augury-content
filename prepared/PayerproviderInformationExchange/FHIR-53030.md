## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept as Persuasive (Approve)

**Rationale:** The request is straightforward IG housekeeping—adding an STU2 section to `ChangeHistory.md` so readers can track what changed between STU1 and STU2. The resolution confirms the change was already implemented and reviewed on the CARIN DIC Community Call on 2025-10-03. Change history pages are standard practice for FHIR IGs and essential for implementer awareness. There is no technical risk or controversy.

**Action:** Accept the applied resolution. No further changes required.

### Disposition B: Accept as Persuasive with Modification

**Rationale:** Accept the change but request that the STU2 section in `ChangeHistory.md` include specific references to the Jira tickets (FHIR-53096, FHIR-53028, FHIR-53034, etc.) that drove the STU2 changes. This would improve traceability for implementers reviewing the change history.

**Action:** Accept with a request to add ticket-level detail or links to the STU2 change history section.

**Rationale:** Defer the ticket until all STU2 changes are finalized to ensure the change history section is comprehensive. Since other STU2 tickets (e.g., FHIR-53034) are still in "Submitted" status, the change history may be incomplete.

**Action:** Defer until remaining STU2 tickets are resolved and the change history can be completed in one pass.

## Recommendation

## Description

The submitter (Joan Harper, imported by Ron G. Parker as Comment 6) requests that the CodeSystem canonical URL for the CARIN Digital Insurance Card IG be anchored in `terminology.hl7.org` (THO) rather than `hl7.org/fhir`. A UTG ticket should be created to register the CodeSystem in THO.

This is a terminology governance issue: HL7 policy expects CodeSystem and ValueSet canonical URLs to be hosted under the Unified Terminology Governance (UTG) managed domain `terminology.hl7.org`, not under the FHIR specification domain `hl7.org/fhir`.

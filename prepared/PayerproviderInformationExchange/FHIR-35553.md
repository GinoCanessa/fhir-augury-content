## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive

**Action:** Accept the request. Move the ValueSet canonical URL from hl7.org/fhir to terminology.hl7.org (THO) and create a corresponding UTG ticket for registration.

**Rationale:** The commenter is correct that ValueSet canonical URLs for IG-defined value sets should be anchored in THO per HL7 terminology governance policy. The sibling ticket FHIR-35559 was already resolved as Persuasive, and FHIR-35551 also received a Persuasive resolution, establishing precedent within this same ticket series. The original deferral rationale (waiting for Vocabulary WG to formalize the process) has had over three years to mature, and the THO registration process is now better established. Continuing to defer creates inconsistency with the sibling tickets that have already been resolved.

**Vote Suggestion:** Persuasive (with appropriate UTG ticket creation)

### Disposition B: Not Persuasive with Modification

**Action:** Partially accept. Evaluate whether the specific ValueSet referenced in Comment 3 is appropriate for THO registration based on current Vocabulary WG criteria. If the ValueSet is IG-specific (not broadly reusable), keep it anchored in the IG's namespace. If it is broadly applicable, migrate it to THO.

**Rationale:** Not all ValueSets need to be in THO. The HL7 policy distinguishes between terminology that is broadly shared across the FHIR ecosystem (which belongs in THO) and IG-specific terminology (which may appropriately remain in the IG's own canonical namespace). The sibling ticket FHIR-35558 was resolved as "Not Persuasive with Modification," suggesting the WG applied this nuanced analysis in at least one case. A case-by-case evaluation ensures correct placement rather than blanket migration.

**Vote Suggestion:** Not Persuasive with Modification (with case-specific analysis documented)

### Disposition C: Considered for Future Use

**Action:** Defer again, pending completion of the broader THO migration effort and formal guidance from the Vocabulary WG.

**Rationale:** This ticket has been deferred twice before with the same rationale. The WG previously voted 11-0-1 to defer, acknowledging the issue but noting that the Vocabulary WG had not yet formalized the process. If the THO registration process and criteria for IG-defined value sets are still not fully clarified, another deferral may be justified. However, this approach risks perpetual deferral of a "Highest" priority Block-Vote item, and the re-openings after each publication cycle suggest the community expects resolution.

**Vote Suggestion:** Considered for Future Use (only if Vocabulary WG process is genuinely still pending)

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Persuasive** is recommended.

This ticket has been open for over three years, deferred twice, and re-opened twice. Sibling tickets FHIR-35559 (Persuasive) and FHIR-35558 (Not Persuasive with Modification) have already been resolved and published, demonstrating that the WG can and has addressed this class of change. The THO registration process is now more mature than when the original deferral was voted. As a "Highest" priority Block-Vote-6 item, continued deferral undermines ballot credibility. The WG should accept the change, create the UTG ticket, and migrate the ValueSet canonical URL to terminology.hl7.org, consistent with the resolution of sibling tickets.

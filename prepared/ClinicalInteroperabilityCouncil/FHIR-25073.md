## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Apply as Persuasive — Add a Diagram Legend
**Action:** Accept the ballot comment and add a color/shape legend to all diagrams in the IG that use visual encoding (colors, shapes, line styles) to convey meaning.
**Rationale:** The resolution already agreed this was persuasive and promised diagramming improvements. Adding a legend is a straightforward usability improvement that helps implementers correctly interpret the specification's visual materials. This is standard practice in technical specifications.
**Risk:** Low. This is a documentation/presentation change with no impact on profiles, value sets, or conformance requirements.

### Disposition B: Apply as Persuasive with Partial Scope — Add Legend to Key Diagrams Only
**Action:** Accept the comment but scope the change to only the most complex or frequently referenced diagrams, rather than all diagrams in the IG.
**Rationale:** Some simpler diagrams may not require a legend if they use only basic shapes. Limiting scope reduces effort while still addressing the core concern.
**Risk:** Low, but may leave some diagrams without adequate explanation if the selection is too narrow.

### Disposition C: Defer — Await Broader Diagramming Overhaul
**Action:** Defer this ticket pending a broader effort to overhaul diagramming across the specification, as hinted at in the resolution statement ("improving our diagramming in a number of ways").
**Rationale:** If a larger diagramming redesign is planned, adding a legend now may result in rework. Better to incorporate the legend as part of the comprehensive diagramming update.
**Risk:** The ticket has been open since 2019 with no applied change. Further deferral risks the improvement never being made.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Apply as Persuasive — Add a Diagram Legend.**

The work group already agreed this was persuasive when it was resolved in November 2019, and the requested change is a low-effort, high-value usability improvement. The ticket has remained in "Triaged" status for over five years without implementation. Applying it now with a clear, complete legend for all diagrams closes a long-standing gap and aligns with the work group's original intent. Deferral is not warranted given the age of the ticket and the simplicity of the change.

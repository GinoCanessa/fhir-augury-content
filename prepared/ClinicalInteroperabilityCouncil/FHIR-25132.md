## Problem Statement

The ballot commenter (Mitra Rocca) reported that the "Logical Models" hyperlink on the IG's navigation page points to `http://hl7.org/fhir/us/breast-radiology/2019SEP/logical.html`, which loads an empty page with no actual content. The commenter notes that logical models appear to be key content for the IG and it is unclear whether the content was never published or the URL is incorrect.

## Existing Wording

> Logical Models.

The hyperlink points to `http://hl7.org/fhir/us/breast-radiology/2019SEP/logical.html`, which has no content.

## Resolution Description (from Jira)

> We agree. Because of tooling issues we were forced to make last minute changes to remove the logical models, and missed the links to the logical models. This will be corrected in the next ballot.

The resolution acknowledges that tooling issues forced removal of logical models before ballot publication, and that the dangling links were inadvertently left in place. The fix is to either restore the logical models content or remove/update the broken links in the next ballot cycle.

## Analysis

This is a straightforward ballot comment about broken/empty content in the 2019 SEP ballot of the US Breast Cancer Radiology Reporting IG. The root cause — last-minute removal of logical models due to tooling issues — was acknowledged by the IG authors in the resolution. Multiple related ballot comments (FHIR-25076, FHIR-25075, FHIR-23891) reported the same or closely related issues with missing content and broken hyperlinks, confirming a systemic problem with the ballot publication rather than an isolated defect.

The resolution ("Persuasive") means the workgroup agreed with the commenter and committed to correcting this in a future ballot. Since subsequent ballots of the IG have occurred (STU1 ballot, etc.), the logical models content may already have been addressed in newer versions. The ticket remains in "Triaged" status, suggesting it has not been formally closed/applied despite the passage of time.

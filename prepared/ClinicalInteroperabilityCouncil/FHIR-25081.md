## Description

The commenter states that the US Breast Cancer Radiology Reporting IG "still requires some work to be more accessible to the audience." This is a general usability and readability comment submitted during the ballot cycle (BC #58), requesting that the IG be improved in its content, structure, and overall clarity so that it is easier for the target audience to understand and use.

## Resolution Description

> We agree. We intend for the next ballot to be much improved in content and structure, and be more easily understood.

The work group accepted this feedback as persuasive, acknowledging that the IG needed improvement in accessibility and committing to enhancing content and structure in the next ballot cycle.

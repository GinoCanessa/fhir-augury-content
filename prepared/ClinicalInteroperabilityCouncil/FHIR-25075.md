## Description

In the "Where to Focus: Clinicians and cancer domain experts" section of the IG, two hyperlinks are incorrectly targeted:

- The **"Modeling approach"** link points to the Breast Radiology Report section instead of the actual modeling approach section.
- The **"logical models"** link also points to the Breast Radiology Report section instead of the logical models section.

The submitter requests these links be corrected to point to the appropriate modeling approach and logical model sections of the IG.

## Resolution Description

> We agree, and will improve the links and description of the links in the next ballot.

The resolution (Persuasive) indicates the committee agreed the links are incorrectly targeted and committed to fixing them and improving the link descriptions in a subsequent ballot cycle.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Apply as Persuasive (Confirm Original Resolution)

Accept the original Persuasive resolution. The two links in the "Where to Focus" section ("Modeling approach" and "logical models") should be corrected to point to their intended target sections rather than the Breast Radiology Report section. Link descriptions should also be improved for clarity.

- **Rationale:** This is a straightforward navigation fix. The committee already agreed to the change in 2019. The links are clearly misdirected, and the fix is low-risk and improves usability for clinicians and domain experts navigating the IG.
- **Risk:** Very low. Only hyperlink `href` values and possibly anchor text are affected. No clinical or structural content changes.

### Disposition B: Apply as Persuasive with Modification — Broaden to Address All Broken Links

Accept the Persuasive resolution but expand scope to also address the closely related FHIR-25076 (null/404 links in the same section) as a single coordinated fix. Both tickets target the same "Where to Focus" section and involve misdirected or broken links.

- **Rationale:** Fixing both tickets together ensures a comprehensive cleanup of the "Where to Focus" navigation section rather than piecemeal fixes.
- **Risk:** Low. Slightly broader scope but still confined to link corrections in one section. Requires coordination with FHIR-25076 disposition.

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive — Defer to Current Ballot Status

Close as Not Persuasive on the basis that the specification has undergone significant structural changes since the 2019 ballot (including multiple subsequent ballot cycles), and the specific link targets may no longer exist in their original form. Require re-evaluation against the current IG structure before applying any fix.

- **Rationale:** The IG has evolved substantially since 2019. The "Where to Focus" section content and link targets may have been reorganized, making the original fix description potentially obsolete.
- **Risk:** Medium. If the links are still broken in the current IG, deferring further delays a known usability issue.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Apply as Persuasive (Confirm Original Resolution)**

This is a clear-cut technical correction — two links point to the wrong sections. The committee already agreed (Persuasive resolution, resolved 2019-11-06) and the fix is trivial. The implementer should verify the current IG structure and correct the link targets accordingly. If FHIR-25076 is reviewed in the same session, the work group may wish to coordinate both fixes together, but each ticket stands on its own as an independent correction.

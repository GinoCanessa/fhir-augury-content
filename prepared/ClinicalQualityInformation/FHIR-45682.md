## Summary

This ticket requests revisions to the short descriptions for `Measure.relatedArtifact:predecessor` and `Measure.relatedArtifact:citation` slices in the CQFM Publishable Measure profile (section 17.53.1.1). The submitter notes these short descriptions are not clear or descriptive of the data element, and proposes reworking them to be more helpful to readers.

**However, the reporter immediately commented that this ticket is an unintentional duplicate and should be closed.** The original ticket is [FHIR-45681](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-45681), which contains identical content and remains open (Triaged, Unresolved).

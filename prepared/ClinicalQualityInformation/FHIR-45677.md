## Summary

This ticket requests two minor grammar/punctuation corrections in the HL7 FHIR® Implementation Guide: Quality Measures, Edition 1 — US Realm:

1. **Section 1.1 (Summary):** Remove an extraneous comma after "The Fast Healthcare Interoperability Resource (FHIR) Quality Measure Implementation Guide (QM IG)," in the first sentence.
2. **Section 1.3.3 (Data Model Standards Landscape):** Under the STU Note, add a missing preposition after "implementers" (e.g., "implementers on").

The ticket title itself contains a misspelling ("Grammer" instead of "Grammar"), though the requested corrections in the IG body are valid editorial improvements.

## Details

The submitter identified two grammatical issues in the Quality Measures IG:

- **Comma removal (Section 1.1):** The first sentence of the Summary section contains a comma after the parenthetical "(QM IG)" that should be removed for improved readability.
- **Missing preposition (Section 1.3.3):** In the STU Note within the Data Model Standards Landscape section, the word "implementers" is missing a following preposition (the submitter suggests "on") to complete the grammatical construction.

Both corrections are minor editorial changes that do not affect the technical content or normative meaning of the specification.

## Description

The submitter identified a typographical error in the PDMP Responder Requirements section of the US Medication FHIR IG. The text reads "Requestor Identity **namely name**" where it should read "Requestor Identity **name**" — the word "namely" appears to be an erroneous insertion that makes the bullet point confusing.

### Existing Wording
> The PDMP Responder SHALL collect the following information about each PDMP Rqquestor which will be used for both auditing and for identifying the requestor uniquely.
> - Requestor Identity **namely name**
> - Requestor Role
> - Requestor DEA, NPI Number
> - Requestor Facility information

### Proposed Wording
> The PDMP Responder SHALL collect the following information about each PDMP Rqquestor which will be used for both auditing and for identifying the requestor uniquely.
> - Requestor Identity **name**
> - Requestor Role
> - Requestor DEA, NPI Number
> - Requestor Facility information

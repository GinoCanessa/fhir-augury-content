## Summary

This is a straightforward editorial correction to the US PDMP FHIR IG. The submitter identified a missing word ("work") in a sentence about ONC environmental scans. The existing text reads "Based on environmental scans and prior performed by ONC…" which is grammatically incomplete. The proposed fix adds the word "work" to read "Based on environmental scans and prior work performed by ONC…".

The ticket was originally submitted in October 2020 as part of a batch import from a ballot comment spreadsheet (comment #7). It was initially resolved as "Considered for Future Use" in January 2023 with a 4-0-0 vote (John Hatem / Tim McNeil) because the IG had been withdrawn. It was subsequently reopened in November 2023 by smrobertson when the PDMP on FHIR project (PSS-2225) was re-initiated.

The resolution is marked as **Persuasive**, meaning the proposed change was accepted as-is.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Persuasive (Accept as-is)
**Action:** Accept the proposed wording change. Change "Based on environmental scans and prior performed by ONC…" to "Based on environmental scans and prior work performed by ONC…" in the applicable section of the US PDMP FHIR IG.

**Rationale:** This is a clear grammatical error — the word "work" is missing, leaving the sentence incomplete and confusing. The fix is minimal, non-controversial, and improves readability. The resolution was previously marked Persuasive and voted on (4-0-0) before the IG was deferred.

### Disposition B: Persuasive with Modification
**Action:** Accept the intent of the change but apply a broader editorial review of the surrounding paragraph/section for any additional wording issues, since this ticket was part of a large batch of editorial comments (PDMP-old-issues).

**Rationale:** Given that many sibling tickets address similar editorial and typo issues in the same IG, it may be more efficient to apply all editorial corrections as a batch rather than addressing them individually. The specific fix proposed here would still be included.

### Disposition C: Not Persuasive
**Action:** Reject the change request.

**Rationale:** There is no strong technical justification for rejection since this is a clear grammatical error. However, if the IG content has been substantially rewritten since the original ballot comment, the specific text may no longer exist, rendering the change moot.

## Recommendation

**Disposition A: Persuasive (Accept as-is)**

This is an unambiguous grammatical fix — a missing word that makes the sentence incomplete. The change has already been reviewed and accepted (voted 4-0-0) in a prior resolution cycle. The resolution is straightforward: insert the word "work" into the identified sentence. No technical or semantic controversy exists. The workgroup should accept this and apply the one-word correction to the IG.

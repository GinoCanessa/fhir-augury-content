## Description

The submitter reports that the hyperlink for "Meaningful Use 2015 §170.302(d)" in the US Medication FHIR Implementation Guide is broken. The existing wording references "Meaningful Use 2015 §170.302(d)" as a certification requirement for EHRs, and the proposed fix is simply to correct the hyperlink.

## Summary

Brian Swinkels proposes adding a new page to the **Overview** section of the
Clinical Order Workflow (COW) FHIR IG that gives readers a brief, conceptual
comparison of how COW / FHIR workflow concepts relate to existing HL7 v2
order exchanges. The intent is purely orientational — a high-level mapping
to help v2-experienced implementers ground themselves in COW terminology —
rather than a detailed message-by-message mapping. The reporter explicitly
calls out that this would **not** duplicate or compete with the work of the
V2-to-FHIR project; it would stay at the conceptual level. A draft already
exists on the COW IG build branch (`BMS-Ballot-Updates`) at
`comparing-with-HL7-v2-orders.html`, which can serve as the starting point
for the new page.

There are no cross-referenced Jira tickets, Zulip threads, or GitHub items
linked to this ticket in the FhirAugury index, so the request stands on its
own.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Add a new narrative page to the Overview / introductory section of the
Clinical Order Workflow (COW) FHIR IG titled along the lines of
"Comparing COW with HL7 v2 Orders". Promote (with light editing) the
existing draft at
`https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/fhir-cow-ig/branches/BMS-Ballot-Updates/en/comparing-with-HL7-v2-orders.html`
into the IG's main authoring branch:

- Place the new page in the IG's Overview group (typical IG layout: a new
  `pages/comparing-with-HL7-v2-orders.md` or equivalent in the COW IG
  authoring root, registered in the IG's table of contents / `menu.xml`
  / `sushi-config.yaml` `pages` block).
- Keep the content **conceptual only**: a short narrative plus a
  high-level table mapping v2 order concepts (e.g., ORM/OML/ORL message
  events, OBR/ORC segments, placer/filler order numbers) to their COW /
  FHIR-workflow analogues (e.g., `ServiceRequest`, `Task`, `Encounter`,
  identifier roles, fulfillment lifecycle).
- Open with a clear scope statement that this page is orientation
  material and explicitly defers detailed mapping to the V2-to-FHIR
  project, with a link to that project.
- Cross-link from the IG's main Overview / introduction page so readers
  arriving from a v2 background can find it.

#### Justification

This is exactly the kind of orientation aid that lowers the on-ramp for
v2-experienced implementers, who are a primary audience for the COW IG.
A draft already exists, the change is documentation-only (no resource,
profile, or constraint changes), and the reporter has pre-emptively
scoped it away from V2-to-FHIR's territory, which removes the most
likely source of cross-project conflict. Cost to implement is low; risk
of breaking implementers is zero.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Adopt the spirit of the request but, rather than authoring a standalone
narrative inside the COW IG, do **both** of the following:

1. Add a short ("one screen") orientation section to the COW IG's
   existing Overview / Introduction page that names the v2 concepts COW
   most often replaces (orders, results, status updates) and gives a
   compact mapping table — but no more than that.
2. For anything beyond that compact mapping, link out to the V2-to-FHIR
   project's order-related mappings (and, if useful, to a coordinated
   page hosted in V2-to-FHIR rather than COW).

This keeps the COW IG focused on its own normative content and avoids
creating a second, parallel mapping artifact that has to be kept in sync
with V2-to-FHIR over time.

#### Justification

A separate full page in COW, even with a "conceptual only" disclaimer,
will inevitably attract pull requests trying to make it more complete,
and will drift from V2-to-FHIR's authoritative mappings. A short inline
orientation block plus an outbound link is cheaper to maintain, harder
to misuse, and reinforces V2-to-FHIR as the single source of truth for
detailed mappings. The trade-off vs. Disposition A is reduced narrative
depth in COW; the upside is no new long-lived maintenance surface.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Decline to add a v2-comparison page to the COW IG. Rationale: the
V2-to-FHIR project already exists specifically to maintain
v2 ↔ FHIR-resource mappings (including orders), so duplicating any
portion of that work — even at a "conceptual only" level — risks
fragmenting the mapping story and creating two artifacts that may
disagree. Instead, point readers to V2-to-FHIR from the COW IG's
existing Overview page via a single sentence and link.

#### Justification

Defensible on a "single source of truth" basis: the HL7 community has
deliberately invested in V2-to-FHIR as the canonical home for v2-to-FHIR
mappings. Even conceptual mappings tend to encode opinions
(which v2 segment "really" corresponds to which FHIR element), and
having those opinions live in a workflow IG rather than in V2-to-FHIR
weakens both. Declining keeps the COW IG focused on its own normative
content. The cost is that v2-experienced readers get less hand-holding
inside the COW IG itself.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** A

The request is narrowly scoped, documentation-only, addresses a real
audience need (v2-experienced implementers landing on the COW IG cold),
and a workable draft already exists on the build branch. The reporter
has pre-empted the obvious objection (overlap with V2-to-FHIR) by
explicitly constraining the page to conceptual orientation. The
maintenance-burden concern raised in Disposition B is real but
manageable with a clear scope banner at the top of the page and an
explicit "for detailed mappings, see V2-to-FHIR" link; that is a lighter
fix than refusing the page outright. Disposition C is the weakest of
the three: it leaves a known onboarding gap unaddressed when the cost
of closing it is essentially editorial. Accept as requested, with the
mandatory scope-banner / V2-to-FHIR cross-link as an editorial
condition.

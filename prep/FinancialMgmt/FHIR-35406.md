## Summary

The reporter requests that the canonical URL of the `AHANUBCRevenueCodes`
ValueSet, currently published under `hl7.org/fhir` in the US CARIN Blue Button
(FHIR) IG, be re-anchored in `terminology.hl7.org` (THO). The reporter further
asks that a corresponding UTG ticket be created to register the ValueSet in
THO. The ticket is part of the CARIN BB STU2 **Block-Vote-6** group and is
flagged with the **Terminology** label. The most recent disposition history
shows the issue was originally voted "Considered for Future Use" on
2022-07-26 (vote 13-0-0, Spears/Conner), pending completion of UP-316 (the
mechanism for publishing "all codes" ValueSets in THO); after STU2
publication the resolution was reverted and the ticket was re-opened
(2022-11-28, lynn_laakso). No other Jira tickets are explicitly linked to
this issue, and no related GitHub items were surfaced.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Move the canonical URL of the `AHANUBCRevenueCodes` ValueSet from
`http://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/ValueSet/AHANUBCRevenueCodes` (or the
equivalent IG-anchored URL) to a `http://terminology.hl7.org/ValueSet/...`
URL by:

1. Filing a UTG (Unified Terminology Governance) change request to
   register the AHA NUBC Revenue Codes ValueSet (preferably as a
   complete "all codes" enumeration of the external NUBC Revenue Codes
   CodeSystem) under THO with a stable canonical URL.
2. After UTG publication, removing the duplicated ValueSet artifact
   from the CARIN BB IG and updating every binding in CARIN BB
   profiles (most notably the institutional EOB profile that references
   the NUBC revenue code element) to point at the THO canonical URL.
3. Coordinating IG release timing so the binding swap lands in the
   first CARIN BB publication that follows the THO registration.

#### Justification

The HL7 terminology policy is explicit that broadly-reusable ValueSets
— particularly those that wrap an external CodeSystem in its entirety —
belong in THO rather than in individual IGs, so other IGs can bind to
the same canonical URL without taking a dependency on CARIN BB. The
ticket's existing comment thread (lynn_laakso, 2022-11-28) and the
prior Block-Vote-6 disposition note both endorse this destination; the
only reason the original 2022-07-26 vote landed at "Considered for
Future Use" was the absence of the THO "all codes" mechanism (UP-316),
which removes the blocker once delivered. Re-anchoring in THO also
aligns FHIR-35406 with the broader CARIN BB Terminology cleanup work
labeled `Terminology` in this block vote.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Keep the ValueSet artifact in the CARIN BB IG for the current STU but
change its canonical URL prefix and metadata so it is clearly a
"placeholder" pointing at the future THO location, and add a normative
note in the IG that the binding will be re-pointed to THO in the next
substantive release once UTG publication completes. Concretely: leave
the artifact in place, update its `ValueSet.url` to a CARIN-namespaced
URL that mirrors the THO naming, add a `ValueSet.copyright` /
narrative note documenting the planned migration, and file the UTG
ticket as a separate work item without blocking this STU on it.

#### Justification

This avoids a hard dependency between the CARIN BB STU release train
and UTG's UP-316 delivery date, which has historically slipped. It
preserves implementer URL stability for the current STU (no broken
binding URLs in the wild) while still telegraphing the intended
direction. The trade-off versus Disposition A is that CARIN BB
continues to carry a duplicate ValueSet artifact for one more cycle
and implementers will need to refactor their bindings later — a real
but bounded cost.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Decline the change for this STU on the basis that the requested
canonical-URL move is *blocked by external work* (UP-316 / UTG
publication of "all codes" ValueSets for external code systems) and
re-anchoring the URL before THO is ready would create a broken
canonical reference. Mark the ticket "Considered for Future Use" again
and re-link UP-316 as the prerequisite, with the explicit understanding
that the request will be re-evaluated automatically once UP-316 is
resolved.

#### Justification

This was in fact the workgroup's prior position (vote 13-0-0 on
2022-07-26) and remains defensible on procedural grounds: re-pointing
the canonical URL before the destination ValueSet exists in THO would
dangle the binding. Declining (or deferring) is honest about the
external dependency and avoids creating churn in the CARIN BB IG that
would have to be revisited if UP-316's eventual canonical URL differs
from what CARIN BB anticipates.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** A

The original 2022 deferral was driven by a missing UTG mechanism
(UP-316), not by any disagreement with the underlying terminology
policy — the workgroup, the reporter, and the announcement thread all
agree the ValueSet should ultimately live in THO. The 2022-11-28
re-open by lynn_laakso reflects that the deferral is no longer
satisfying the workgroup, and continuing to ship a CARIN-anchored
duplicate of an externally-owned code system is exactly the kind of
fragmentation HL7's terminology guidance is trying to retire.
Disposition A directly executes that policy by filing the UTG ticket
and switching the binding once THO publication completes; sequencing
the IG change behind the UTG publication eliminates the broken-URL risk
that motivated Disposition C, and avoids the "placeholder URL" debt
that Disposition B would accumulate. The workgroup should accept the
change with the explicit gating condition that the binding swap lands
only after the THO ValueSet is published.

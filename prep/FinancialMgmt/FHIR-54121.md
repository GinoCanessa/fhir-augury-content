## Summary

Brett Marquard recommends that the three financial-domain resources
**Invoice**, **InsuranceProduct**, and **InsurancePlan** be moved out of
the FHIR R6 core specification and reclassified as **Additional
Resources** (i.e., published via an incubator IG rather than as part of
the normative/core ballot package). The reporter states he is unaware of
substantive system support or testing of these three resources at R6
ballot4. The ticket is the "umbrella" recommendation; a sibling ticket,
[FHIR-54124](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-54124), narrowly notes
that **InsuranceProduct** is missing from the Jira Related Artifacts
list and makes the same Move-to-Additional recommendation. FHIR-54124
has already been resolved as **Not Persuasive with Modification** with
the rationale that Invoice is in regulatory use in Germany and that
InsuranceProduct/InsurancePlan represent an intentional R6 split of the
R4 InsurancePlan resource into "risk/costs" (InsurancePlan) and
"benefits" (InsuranceProduct); removal will be re-assessed in spring
2026. Comments from Corey Spears confirm the recommendation applies to
**all three** resources and that ownership has been moved from FHIR-I
to Financial Management. FHIR-54121 is the higher-priority "Highest"
companion intended to force a Financial Management-owned decision on
the same question.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Withdraw **Invoice**, **InsuranceProduct**, and **InsurancePlan** from
the R6 FHIR Core ballot package and republish them as Additional
Resources via an incubator IG (the existing
[HL7/admin-incubator](https://build.fhir.org/ig/HL7/admin-incubator)
where Brian Postlethwaite has already staged them is the natural home
for the two insurance resources; Invoice would either join the same
incubator or move to a finance-specific incubator IG owned by FM).
Concretely, in the R6 source build remove the `Invoice`,
`InsuranceProduct`, and `InsurancePlan` entries from the resource list
(and from Jira Related Artifacts so FHIR-54124's surface concern
disappears in passing), and add them as `additional-resource` entries
in the relevant incubator IG's `sushi-config.yaml` (the same pattern
documented in the FMG incubator thread).

#### Justification

The reporter's premise — limited demonstrated implementation/testing
at R6 ballot — is independently supported by the R6 ballot4 publication
itself, which carries a "Note to Balloters" on InsuranceProduct
explicitly flagging the resource as a removal candidate (see Reinhard
Egelkraut's reference in the PA "Account in R6" thread). PA has
already acted on the joint PA/FM Pittsburgh decision to relocate
InsurancePlan and InsuranceProduct to the admin incubator, so the
ground-truth state of the R6 build is already inconsistent with
"keep in core". Grahame Grieve's review of Invoice in the incubator
thread (mis-spelled `DEPRICATED` short, malformed definition referring
to a non-existent element) is direct evidence that Invoice is not
ballot-ready normative content. Pulling all three to Additional
Resources resolves the cross-WG disagreement between the 28-Oct FMG
minutes ("stay in R6") and PA's behavior, and aligns the published
ballot with FMG's stated intent to give immature resources room to
mature in incubators.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Keep **Invoice** in R6 core but with a clear "Note to Balloters" stating
that Invoice's normative status will be re-assessed in spring 2026, and
fix the obvious editorial defects (mis-spelled `DEPRICATED` short,
malformed definition) before publication. Move **InsuranceProduct** and
**InsurancePlan** to the `HL7/admin-incubator` Additional Resources IG
(ratifying what PA has already done), and update the Jira Related
Artifacts list (closing the surface defect in FHIR-54124) so the two
removed resources are no longer offered as core artifacts. Have FM
formally adopt the existing admin-incubator location for these two
resources rather than spinning up a finance-specific incubator.

#### Justification

This split disposition reflects the actual evidence per resource rather
than treating all three identically. Invoice has the strongest
real-world implementation story (national use in Germany per the
FHIR-54124 resolution and German/Indian profiles per the incubator
thread), so removal would be disproportionate; a "remove later if
unimproved" Note to Balloters preserves implementer signal without
blocking R6. InsurancePlan/InsuranceProduct are demonstrably
under-implemented at R6 (only the legacy R4 InsurancePlan has
deployment evidence), and PA has already physically moved them out of
core in the admin-incubator, so leaving them in the R6 ballot creates
an active inconsistency between the build site and the ballot
package. This option also avoids creating duplicate/competing
incubator IGs, which the FMG thread explicitly warned against (Brian
Postlethwaite's request for a common incubator naming format
underscored the discoverability cost of fragmenting them).

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Decline FHIR-54121 (and let FHIR-54124's existing "Not Persuasive with
Modification" disposition stand) on the basis that the question has
already been answered by FMG: per the 28-Oct-25 FMG minutes,
InsurancePlan, InsuranceProduct, and Invoice will remain in R6 core for
ballot, and any removal will be re-assessed in spring '26 once
ballot-comment evidence is in. The action item under this disposition
is editorial only: ensure InsuranceProduct appears in the Jira Related
Artifacts list (closing the FHIR-54124 surface defect) and confirm the
"Note to Balloters" already on InsuranceProduct is also added to
InsurancePlan and Invoice so balloters have a clear signal that
post-ballot removal is on the table.

#### Justification

Removing three resources from a ballot package late in the cycle is a
disruptive action that prejudges the very evidence the ballot is
designed to surface. The FHIR-54124 resolution already articulates the
defensible rationale: Invoice has demonstrated regulatory use
(Germany); the InsurancePlan/InsuranceProduct split is an intentional
R6 design that materially differs from the R4 InsurancePlan, so "lack
of R6 implementation evidence" is the expected state at first ballot,
not a sign the resources should be withdrawn; and a structured spring
'26 reassessment with ballot-comment data in hand will be a better
basis for a removal decision than a January preemptive move. The
"Note to Balloters" mechanism already in use on InsuranceProduct
gives implementers all the information they need without modifying
the ballot package.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

Disposition B is the recommended starting point for the workgroup.
Disposition C is procedurally tidy but ignores ground truth: PA has
already moved InsurancePlan/InsuranceProduct into `HL7/admin-incubator`
on the build site, so the published R6 ballot artifact and the working
incubator IG will diverge unless FM acts. Disposition A is consistent
but treats Invoice the same as the two insurance resources, which the
evidence does not support — Invoice has documented German national use
and Indian profiles, which is exactly the kind of implementation
signal that justifies keeping a resource in core (modulo cleaning up
the editorial defects Grahame flagged). Disposition B ratifies what
PA has already done for the two insurance resources, fixes the
FHIR-54124 surface defect in passing (Jira Related Artifacts), keeps
Invoice in R6 with a transparent re-assessment commitment, and avoids
the discoverability cost of a third "finance-only" incubator IG by
co-locating with admin-incubator. The workgroup should explicitly
record the joint PA/FM decision so the discrepancy between the
28-Oct-25 FMG minutes and the PA-side action is resolved on the record
rather than left as folklore.

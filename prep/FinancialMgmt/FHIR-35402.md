## Summary

This ticket asserts that the canonical defining URL for the CARIN BB IG
artifact `ValueSet-AHANUBCPatientDischargeStatus` should be anchored in
`terminology.hl7.org` (THO) rather than under `hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb`,
because only ValueSets that are directly referenced by a `code`-typed
element with a `required` binding should remain anchored under an IG's
own canonical. The reporter notes that a UTG ticket should be opened to
register the value set in THO. The issue was originally resolved
"Considered for Future Use" in Block-Vote-6 on 2022-07-26 (vote
13-0-0) on the basis that the ValueSet is an "all codes" enumeration of
an external CodeSystem, and that hosting in THO depends on the
cross-cutting [UP-316](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-316) (Create "All
Codes" ValueSets for all External CodeSystems). Lynn Laakso re-opened
the ticket post STU2 publication on 2022-11-28, reverting the prior
resolution; it remains in `Triaged` for Financial Mgmt to dispose of.
Related, already-Published CARIN BB clean-up work
([FHIR-38285](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-38285),
[FHIR-38763](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-38763)) removed
duplicative IG-defined CodeSystem stubs and pointed CodeSystem
references at the canonical NUBC URL
(`https://www.nubc.org/CodeSystem/PatDischargeStatus`); the still-open
[FHIR-48340](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-48340) ("Submit value sets
to THO") is the active Financial Mgmt vehicle for moving CARIN BB value
sets — including the NUBC discharge-status set — to THO.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Accept the request and migrate `ValueSet-AHANUBCPatientDischargeStatus`
out of `hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb` and into THO. Concretely:

1. File (or link to) a UTG/THO request to publish the "all codes"
   ValueSet for the NUBC Patient Discharge Status CodeSystem in THO at
   `http://terminology.hl7.org/ValueSet/<thoName>` (the THO governance
   process under [UP-316](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-316) determines
   the exact slug and hosting pattern).
2. In the next CARIN BB publication, remove the locally-defined
   `ValueSet-AHANUBCPatientDischargeStatus` resource and replace every
   binding that currently references
   `https://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/ValueSet/AHANUBCPatientDischargeStatus`
   with the new THO canonical (e.g., on the
   `ExplanationOfBenefit-Outpatient-Institutional` and related
   institutional EOB profiles where Patient Discharge Status is bound).
3. Treat this ticket as a sub-task of
   [FHIR-48340](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-48340) (the active
   "submit value sets to THO" umbrella) and close FHIR-35402 as
   `Persuasive` once the THO ValueSet is published and the IG bindings
   are re-pointed.

#### Justification

This is exactly the pattern Lynn Laakso preserved in her 2022-11-28
re-open comment, and it is consistent with the precedent the workgroup
already set in the Persuasive/Published tickets
[FHIR-38285](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-38285) (CodeSystem stubs
removed in favour of THO) and
[FHIR-38763](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-38763) (CodeSystem
references re-pointed to canonical NUBC URLs). It also aligns with the
stated FHIR/HL7 terminology guidance that an IG should not anchor
"all-codes" enumerations of external code systems under the IG's own
canonical when a shared THO home is available. The dependency on
UP-316/FHIR-48340 is real but is now the active path forward rather
than a blocker, so adopting Disposition A is a forward-looking
commitment rather than speculative work.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Persuasive With Modification: rather than block FHIR-35402 on the THO
hosting work, change CARIN BB's bindings now to **reference the
external (NUBC) ValueSet directly by its canonical** wherever the
binding strength permits, and retain a thin IG-local ValueSet only
where required by a `code`-typed element with a `required` binding (the
exact carve-out the ticket text proposes). Concretely:

1. Inspect every place in CARIN BB that binds to
   `ValueSet-AHANUBCPatientDischargeStatus`. For non-`required`
   bindings on `Coding`/`CodeableConcept` elements (the common case for
   discharge status on institutional EOB profiles), re-point the
   binding directly at the NUBC code-system canonical via an
   `ImplicitValueSet` or an `extensible` binding to the THO-hosted
   ValueSet name once it exists; if THO hosting is not yet ready,
   document the intent in the IG narrative.
2. For any binding that is `required` and against a `code` data type
   (per the ticket's own carve-out), keep the locally-defined
   `ValueSet-AHANUBCPatientDischargeStatus` exactly as it is today.
3. Resolve FHIR-35402 once both pieces are reflected in the next CARIN
   BB publication, with explicit cross-references to UP-316 and
   FHIR-48340.

#### Justification

This honours the spirit of the ticket (anchor canonicals in THO; only
keep IG-local definitions where binding rules force it) while not
making CARIN BB's resolution wait on the broader UP-316 effort. It also
matches how Financial Mgmt has already disposed of the sister
CodeSystem tickets (FHIR-38285/FHIR-38763), where references were
moved without waiting for a perfect cross-spec hosting solution.
Trade-offs versus Disposition A: it ships a smaller, IG-scoped change
faster, but it leaves a tiny IG-local ValueSet stub in place until UP-316
actually lands, which is a slight inconsistency with the "no IG-local
mirrors of THO content" direction.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Persuasive With Modification → effectively **Considered for Future Use
again**: re-affirm the original Block-Vote-6 resolution and decline to
take action in CARIN BB until UP-316 produces the THO "all codes"
ValueSet, then close FHIR-35402 as a duplicate of
[FHIR-48340](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-48340) (which is already
Triaged at Financial Mgmt and explicitly names the
`AHANUBCPatientDischargeStatus` ValueSet).

#### Justification

The substantive work — registering the value set in THO and re-pointing
CARIN BB bindings — is already captured in
[FHIR-48340](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-48340), which has a
broader scope (multiple value sets) and is the natural place for
Financial Mgmt to track the migration. Keeping FHIR-35402 open as a
parallel single-artifact ticket invites duplicate effort and divergent
resolutions. The earlier 13-0-0 vote shows the workgroup already
favoured deferring; the only reason it was re-opened was procedural
(post-STU2 timing), not because the substantive analysis changed.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** A (with explicit linkage to FHIR-48340).

The ticket asks for a specific, well-scoped change whose direction is
no longer in doubt: CARIN BB has already been migrating away from
IG-local mirrors of external terminology (FHIR-38285, FHIR-38763 both
landed Persuasive/Published), and Financial Mgmt has an open,
in-progress ticket — [FHIR-48340](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-48340) —
that already names this exact ValueSet as in-scope for THO submission.
Accepting FHIR-35402 as requested, while treating it as a sub-task of
FHIR-48340, gives the workgroup a clean disposition record without
losing the artifact-specific traceability that the original reporter
asked for. Disposition B is a reasonable fallback if the workgroup
feels UP-316/THO timing is too uncertain to commit, but it leaves a
known-temporary local ValueSet in place; Disposition C is the lowest
effort but risks a third round of "re-open post next publication" if
the umbrella ticket slips.

## Summary

The ticket asks that the canonical (defining) URL for the PCT
`PCTSupportingInfoType` CodeSystem be anchored under
`terminology.hl7.org` (THO) rather than under the PCT IG's own
`hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-pct` namespace, and that a corresponding UTG
ticket be opened to register the CodeSystem in THO. The motivation is
that the concept set is broadly applicable to claim/EOB
`supportingInfo.category` use across multiple Financial Mgmt IGs (PCT,
CARIN BB, Da Vinci PAS) and is therefore a poor fit for an IG-local
CodeSystem. The ticket has been resolved twice and re-opened twice
since 2022 — first as "Considered for Future Use" in 2022-10-25
(re-opened post-STU1 in 2023), and again as "Considered for Future
Use" in the 2025-07-08 PCT block vote (re-opened post-STU2 on
2025-10-08). The current path forward is the broader
cross-IG/THO harmonisation effort tracked by UTG ticket
[UP-691](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-691) — "Update Claim
Information Category Codes CodeSystem to incorporate codes from
multiple IGs" — which proposes a single THO-hosted CodeSystem covering
PCT, CARIN BB (`C4BBSupportingInfoType`, plus PCT's `cmspos` code) and
PAS (LOINC attachment codes). This ticket should now be dispositioned
in the context of UP-691 rather than as a standalone PCT-only move.

### [UP-691: Update Claim Information Category Codes CodeSystem to incorporate codes from multiple IGs](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-691)

The active UTG proposal that this ticket now depends on. UP-691
proposes a single THO-hosted "Claim Information Category Codes"
CodeSystem that consolidates the PCT `PCTSupportingInfoType` codes
(including PCT-specific `cmspos`), CARIN BB's
`C4BBSupportingInfoType`, and PAS LOINC attachment codes, with
follow-on cleanup (e.g., fix `roudtrippurpose` → `roundtrippurpose`,
move to kebab-case). Discussion on UP-691 also raises a modelling
question about whether some of these codes really belong on
`supportingInfo.code` (with category set to a generic `attachment`)
rather than on `supportingInfo.category` itself — which would change
how PCT consumes the resulting CodeSystem.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Move `PCTSupportingInfoType` out of the PCT IG and re-host it under
`http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/PCTSupportingInfoType` (or an
equivalent THO slug). Concretely: (1) in UTG, register a new
THO-anchored CodeSystem with the existing concept set (preserving
codes, displays, and definitions, including `cmspos`); (2) in the PCT
IG, replace the local CodeSystem definition with a reference to the
THO canonical, retire the in-IG CodeSystem resource, and re-point all
PCT ValueSets that include `PCTSupportingInfoType` (and any profile
bindings that name the local URL) at the THO canonical; (3) treat the
old PCT canonical as a deprecated alias and document the move in the
PCT IG change log. Track the UTG side under
[UP-691](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-691) — but scoped narrowly to
the PCT codes only, not the broader cross-IG harmonisation.

#### Justification

This is what the ticket literally asks for and aligns with the FHIR
methodology guidance that CodeSystems with reuse potential should
live in THO rather than in an IG-local namespace. The same
disposition has already been applied to sibling PCT tickets in the
2025-07 block vote where the relevant codes were judged stable enough
to move (e.g., FHIR-35549, FHIR-35545, FHIR-35540 — all "Persuasive,
Move to THO"). The downside is that it does **not** address Corey
Spears' substantive concern that this CodeSystem overlaps with CARIN
BB's `C4BBSupportingInfoType` and PAS attachment codes, so a
PCT-only THO move could still leave the FM portfolio with three
near-duplicate CodeSystems.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Resolve **Persuasive with Modification — defer to UP-691**: do not
move `PCTSupportingInfoType` as-is, and instead retire it in favour
of the consolidated THO-hosted "Claim Information Category Codes"
CodeSystem proposed by [UP-691](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-691).
Concretely: (1) keep `PCTSupportingInfoType` in place for the current
PCT publication; (2) once UP-691 is accepted into THO, re-bind PCT's
ValueSets and profiles (`PCTGFEItemInstitutional`,
`PCTGFEItemProfessional`, etc., wherever
`Claim/ExplanationOfBenefit.supportingInfo.category` /
`supportingInfo.code` is bound) to the THO-anchored value sets
derived from the consolidated CodeSystem; (3) settle the open
modelling question on UP-691 (whether these codes belong on
`supportingInfo.category` or on `supportingInfo.code` with a generic
`attachment` category) before re-binding. Mark this ticket as a
dependency on UP-691 so it cannot be closed independently.

#### Justification

This is the path the FM workgroup has already been steering toward
for three years: the 2022 and 2025 block votes both deferred the
move on the grounds that the codes need cross-IG harmonisation
first, and UP-691 is the concrete vehicle for that. Moving
PCT-only now (Disposition A) would create churn — first a PCT→THO
canonical change, then a second THO→THO consolidation when UP-691
lands — and risks freezing PCT-specific encodings (e.g., `cmspos`,
the modelling of service dates) that may need to change as part of
the harmonisation. The trade-off is timing: PCT consumers will
continue to see an IG-local canonical until UP-691 ships, which
delays the THO benefit but avoids a double migration.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Resolve **Not Persuasive**: keep `PCTSupportingInfoType` defined in
the PCT IG at its current `hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-pct/...`
canonical and do not promote it to THO. Close the ticket with a
note that the existing IG-local CodeSystem is fit for purpose for
PCT and that any cross-IG harmonisation should be pursued as a
separate workstream that does not block PCT publishing.

#### Justification

Defensible only if the workgroup concludes that PCT's use of these
codes is sufficiently divergent from CARIN BB / PAS that there is
no realistic shared CodeSystem (i.e., harmonisation is not
achievable). Given that UP-691 already exists, UTG work is
underway, and Lynn Laakso explicitly re-opened this ticket post-STU2
to keep it tied to the FM/THO effort, declining outright would
contradict the workgroup's stated direction and would likely be
re-opened a fourth time. Recommended only as a fallback if UP-691
is itself withdrawn.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

The history is unambiguous: the workgroup has twice resolved this
ticket as "Considered for Future Use" specifically because the
correct answer is cross-IG harmonisation, not a PCT-only THO move,
and Lynn Laakso re-opened the ticket post-STU2 explicitly to bind
it to the broader FM/THO effort that UP-691 now embodies. Disposition
A would technically satisfy the literal request but would force a
second migration once UP-691 lands and would lock in PCT-specific
encodings that are still under active discussion (notably whether
the codes belong on `supportingInfo.category` or
`supportingInfo.code`, and the fate of `cmspos`). Disposition C
ignores the FM workgroup's clearly-stated direction. Disposition B
preserves the original intent of the ticket (move to THO), addresses
the substantive harmonisation concern raised on UP-691, and gives
the workgroup a clean way to track the dependency rather than
deferring the ticket a third time.

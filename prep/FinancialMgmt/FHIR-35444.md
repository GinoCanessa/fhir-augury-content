## Summary

FHIR-35444 asks the CARIN IG for Blue Button® (C4BB) maintainers to move the
canonical (defining) URL of `ValueSet-NCPDPPrescriptionOriginCode` out of the
IG's own URL space (`http://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/...`) and instead anchor
it under terminology.hl7.org (THO), so it can be governed centrally as
terminology rather than as IG-local content. The reporter notes that a
companion UTG ticket should be filed to register the value set in THO. The
ticket is one of ~25 sibling change requests filed by Joan Harper at the same
time (FHIR-35426 through FHIR-35447, plus related ones), each targeting a
different C4BB-defined CodeSystem or ValueSet, and is part of CARIN BB
STU2 **Block-Vote-6**. The ticket was originally resolved
**Considered for Future Use** in the 2022-07-26 block vote with the
disposition "Plan on making change when external CodeSystem 'all codes'
ValueSet is published ([UP-316](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-316))," but
was subsequently reverted and re-opened post-STU2 publication, so it is
again open for disposition.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Relocate `ValueSet-NCPDPPrescriptionOriginCode` out of the C4BB IG and
publish it under `http://terminology.hl7.org/ValueSet/...` (THO). Concretely:
(1) file/advance a UTG ticket to register an "all codes from
NCPDPPrescriptionOriginCode" ValueSet in THO with a stable
`terminology.hl7.org` canonical; (2) once the THO ValueSet is published,
remove the IG-local `ValueSet-NCPDPPrescriptionOriginCode` definition from
the C4BB IG; (3) repoint any C4BB profile bindings (e.g., on
`MedicationDispense`/`MedicationRequest` or the C4BB pharmacy EOB profile
that consumes prescription-origin) to the new THO canonical; and
(4) record the canonical change in the IG migration / cross-version notes
for STU2.x consumers.

#### Justification

Anchoring complete-CodeSystem-wrap ValueSets in THO is the established HL7
governance pattern: it makes the ValueSet reusable across IGs (CARIN,
Da Vinci, US Core successors), avoids each IG re-publishing identical
"all codes" wrappers, and matches the WG's stated long-term direction in
the Block-Vote-6 announcement and the [UP-316](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-316)
strategy. The reporter's concern (Comment 56, Ron G. Parker) is exactly
the consistency complaint that THO governance is meant to resolve. The
re-open by Lynn Laakso post-STU2 publication signals the WG is now willing
to revisit, presumably because UP-316 / "all codes ValueSet" infrastructure
has matured.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach — Defer until UP-316 lands, then auto-adopt

#### Proposal

Keep the IG-local `ValueSet-NCPDPPrescriptionOriginCode` in C4BB at its
current canonical for the next publication, but (a) explicitly mark the
ValueSet as deprecated-in-place with a narrative pointer to the planned
THO ValueSet, (b) add a tracking dependency on
[UP-316](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-316) (and on the THO publication
of the NCPDP "all codes" ValueSet) so that as soon as the THO ValueSet
ships, C4BB can swap the binding canonical and remove the local copy in a
single patch release, and (c) leave bindings unchanged in this cycle to
avoid breaking existing implementations.

#### Justification

This is the disposition the WG already converged on in the original 2022
block vote ("Considered for Future Use, plan on making change when external
CodeSystem 'all codes' ValueSet is published (UP-316)"). The technical
prerequisite (a generic THO mechanism for complete external CodeSystems)
either still depends on UP-316 or on confirmation that UP-316 has shipped.
Acting before that infrastructure exists risks publishing a one-off THO
ValueSet that doesn't follow the eventual UP-316 pattern, producing churn
again. This disposition trades a short-term consistency wart for a clean,
batch-style migration aligned with all the sibling tickets
(FHIR-35441–35445 and FHIR-35440), keeping the family coherent.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Resolve **Not Persuasive**: keep `ValueSet-NCPDPPrescriptionOriginCode`
defined in the C4BB IG with its current `hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb`
canonical. Document in the IG that NCPDP-derived ValueSets used by C4BB
will continue to be IG-local because NCPDP content is licensed/external and
its publication cadence is not under HL7 control, and that re-anchoring in
THO does not change the content the implementer sees.

#### Justification

This is the same rationale the WG applied to several siblings in the same
block vote (e.g., FHIR-35432, FHIR-35434, FHIR-35435, FHIR-35436,
FHIR-35439, FHIR-35446, FHIR-35447 — all "Not Persuasive" because the
ValueSet is "not complex, fairly specific to this IG, and does not warrant
the maintenance of moving to THO"). For a thin "all codes from external
CodeSystem" wrapper, moving the canonical adds governance overhead in THO
without changing semantics, and the canonical URL is opaque to most
downstream implementers as long as the binding resolves. Declining keeps
STU2.x stable and avoids waiting on UP-316.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

Disposition B is the cleanest path because it is consistent with the way
the WG has already chosen to handle every sibling ValueSet in this block
that wraps a complete external CodeSystem (FHIR-35440 through FHIR-35445),
and consistent with the original 13-0-0 vote on FHIR-35444 itself. The
re-open is not a signal that the original reasoning was wrong; it is a
signal that the WG wants to re-confirm the plan now that STU2 has
published. Disposition A is right *eventually* but acting on it before
UP-316 / the THO "all codes" mechanism ships risks creating a one-off THO
ValueSet that has to be re-canonicalized again later. Disposition C
under-serves the reporter's terminology-governance concern and breaks
parity with how the WG treated the rest of the NCPDP/AHA/CMS family.
Recommend resolving as **Considered for Future Use** with an explicit link
to [UP-316](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/UP-316), and tracking the swap as
a planned post-UP-316 patch.

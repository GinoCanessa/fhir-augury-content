## Summary

Joan Harper (originally raised by Ron G. Parker via comment import) asks
that the canonical/defining URL of the PCT IG's `PCTProcedureType` Code
System be anchored in `terminology.hl7.org` (THO) rather than under
`hl7.org/fhir`, and that a UTG ticket be opened to register the Code
System in THO. The request is one of a family of "Defining URL should be
anchored in THO" tickets against the PCT IG raised by the same reporter
(e.g., FHIR-35537/35540/35545/35549/35550, etc.), all included in the
PCT Block-2025-Jul block vote.

The ticket has gone through several reopen/revert cycles since 2022.
Most recently, related ticket
[FHIR-50882](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-50882) (Resolved /
Persuasive, May 2025) approved creating a non-example CodeSystem and
ValueSet to replace the FHIR core `Ex-ProcedureType` example, and a
UTG submission was drafted under UP-686 with a candidate THO Code
System (`procedure-type`). On 2025-07-08 the PCT block vote
(documented in the Da Vinci PCT Zulip thread) carried 9-0-2 with a
disposition of "Considered for Future Use / Resolve across IGs
continuing to add to THO". That resolution was then reverted on
2025-10-08 by Lynn Laakso ("re-open post STU2 publication") because
concerns were raised about where the codes should live and what the
codes/definitions should be at the broader scope, so the ticket is
back in **Triaged** status awaiting a fresh disposition.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Move the `PCTProcedureType` CodeSystem and `PCTProcedureTypeVS`
ValueSet out of the PCT IG and onto THO, on the back of FHIR-50882 /
UP-686:

- Adopt the THO `procedure-type` CodeSystem proposed under UP-686
  (currently previewed at
  `http://utg-submitter-builds.hl7.org:9876/UP-686/site/CodeSystem-procedure-type.html`)
  as the canonical home, with canonical URL anchored under
  `http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/...`.
- In the PCT IG, retire the locally-defined CodeSystem
  `http://hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-pct/CodeSystem/PCTProcedureType`
  and re-point `PCTProcedureTypeVS` (and any profile bindings on
  `Claim.procedure.type` / `ExplanationOfBenefit.procedure.type` in
  PCT) to the new THO CodeSystem / the FHIR-50882 ValueSet.
- Confirm the UTG ticket exists (UP-686) and is on track; this ticket
  itself does not need a separate UTG ticket since UP-686 already
  covers the registration.

#### Justification

This is what the reporter literally asked for and is consistent with
the long-standing HL7 direction that broadly-reusable terminology
should live in THO, not in IG-local namespaces. The PCT community was
recorded as in agreement with the move (2025-06-05 comment from
Corey Spears), the upstream core change to support it has already
been resolved Persuasive (FHIR-50882), and a draft THO CodeSystem is
already published under UP-686. The block vote on 2025-07-08 carried
9-0-2 in favour of resolving across IGs "continuing to add to THO,"
which signals workgroup-level support even though that specific
resolution was administratively reverted post-STU2 to allow
re-evaluation of scope.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Defer the THO migration for `PCTProcedureType` until the broader
scope question raised in the 2025-10-08 reopen comment is settled,
and in the interim:

- Keep `PCTProcedureType` and `PCTProcedureTypeVS` in the PCT IG as
  they are today, with their existing `hl7.org/fhir/us/davinci-pct/...`
  canonicals (no breaking change for STU2 implementers).
- Track the THO migration as a single coordinated cross-IG effort
  rather than per-CodeSystem tickets — i.e., wait for FHIR-50882's
  ValueSet/CodeSystem to publish in THO and be used by the FHIR core
  bindings, then in the next PCT release re-point
  `PCTProcedureTypeVS` to the THO ValueSet and remove the local
  CodeSystem in one coordinated PR alongside the sibling tickets
  (FHIR-35529/32/33/34/35/36/37/40/45/49/50).
- Document the deprecation intent in the PCT IG narrative and on the
  PCTProcedureType / PCTProcedureTypeVS pages so implementers know
  the local URL is transitional.

#### Justification

This matches the direction implicit in the 2025-10-08 reopen note:
"some concern was raised as to where these codes should reside and
what the codes and definitions should be for the larger scope. More
work will need to be done." Splitting the decision (keep canonicals
stable now, migrate as a coordinated batch later) avoids churning the
canonical URL of an already-published STU2 CodeSystem before the THO
home is finalized, and avoids leaving PCTProcedureType "half migrated"
relative to its peer CodeSystems in the same block. It also keeps the
PCT change tightly coupled to FHIR-50882's actual publication in THO,
which is the real prerequisite.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Resolve **Not Persuasive** (or **Considered for Future Use**) on the
basis that THO migration of `PCTProcedureType` is no longer in scope
for the PCT IG itself. Rationale category: *already addressed by
existing mechanism* — FHIR-50882 has been resolved Persuasive in FHIR
core to create a non-example procedure-type CodeSystem/ValueSet and
re-bind `Claim.procedure.type` / `ExplanationOfBenefit.procedure.type`,
and UP-686 owns the THO registration. PCT can simply consume the new
core ValueSet when it is published; no PCT-specific change is needed
beyond eventually re-binding.

#### Justification

The original ticket was filed in 2022 against an early version of PCT
and pre-dated the core/UTG work that now solves the underlying
problem. With FHIR-50882 owning the new procedure-type ValueSet in
core and UP-686 owning the THO CodeSystem, keeping a separate PCT
ticket open creates parallel tracking with no independent action: the
PCT IG's only remaining job is to bind to the new core/THO artifact
when it ships, which is normal IG-version maintenance and not a
ballotable change request. Closing this ticket avoids re-litigating
the scope question raised in the 2025-10-08 reopen at the PCT level
when the right venue is FM core / UTG.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

The reopen on 2025-10-08 is explicit that the workgroup is not yet
comfortable that the THO target (codes, definitions, owning project)
is settled — exactly the situation Disposition B is designed for.
Disposition A is the "right destination," but acting on it now means
changing the canonical URL of a CodeSystem that has just been
published in STU2 before its THO replacement is final, which risks
implementers chasing two different canonical URLs in successive
publications. Disposition C is too dismissive: PCT does have to make
a real (if mechanical) change once FHIR-50882/UP-686 land — re-pointing
`PCTProcedureTypeVS` and dropping the local `PCTProcedureType` —
and this ticket is the natural carrier for that work. Resolving B
keeps the canonical stable for STU2, ties the PCT-side change to the
actual THO publication, and aligns this ticket with the disposition
already chosen for its sibling "anchor in THO" tickets in the
Block-2025-Jul vote.

## Summary

FHIR-35425 asks the CARIN Consumer Directed Payer Data Exchange (Blue Button)
IG team to relocate the canonical (defining) URL of the
`ValueSet-C4BBPayerBenefitPaymentStatus` ValueSet so that it is anchored in
terminology.hl7.org (THO) rather than under `hl7.org/fhir`. The reporter
(Joan Harper, comment 37 imported by Ron G. Parker) notes the general
principle that only ValueSets bound as `required` to a `code` data type
belong under `hl7.fhir.org`; everything else — including this one — should
live in THO. A companion UTG ticket would be required to register the
ValueSet in THO. The ticket sits in the broader Block-Vote-6 sweep of
twenty-six analogous CARIN BB CodeSystem/ValueSet "anchor in THO" tickets
(e.g., FHIR-35390 through FHIR-35447, plus FHIR-36486/36487 and the
related FHIR-37706/37726/37727/37728 cluster). On 2022-07-26 the FM WG
voted 13-0-0 (Spears/Conner) to disposition this item as "Considered for
Future Use" — "May move this ValueSet to THO for a future version based
on harmonization work with other IGs." Lynn Laakso reverted that
resolution on 2022-11-28 and re-opened the ticket post-STU2 publication,
which is why it is currently back in Triaged.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Move the canonical URL of `ValueSet-C4BBPayerBenefitPaymentStatus` from
`http://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/ValueSet/C4BBPayerBenefitPaymentStatus`
to a THO-anchored canonical such as
`http://terminology.hl7.org/ValueSet/C4BBPayerBenefitPaymentStatus`. Concretely:

1. Open a UTG (HL7/UTG) proposal to register the ValueSet (and any
   companion CodeSystem(s) it composes that are not yet in THO) under
   `terminology.hl7.org`, with the same identifier, semantics, codes,
   and metadata.
2. In the CARIN BB IG (`HL7/carin-bb`), update the ValueSet resource
   `url` to the new THO canonical, retain the original canonical as a
   `ValueSet.identifier` for traceability, and bump the artifact's
   business version.
3. Update every binding pointing at the old canonical
   (notably `ExplanationOfBenefit.supportingInfo` /
   `adjudication[benefitpaymentstatus]` slices on the C4BB EOB profiles
   — keeping in mind FHIR-37706/37726 moved benefitpaymentstatus from
   `supportingInfo` to `adjudication`) and any narrative / page text
   that references the old URL.
4. Coordinate publication so the THO release precedes (or accompanies)
   the next CARIN BB ballot, and add a `Comparison` entry / migration
   note for implementers in the IG history page.

#### Justification

This is the literal request and is consistent with the FHIR product family
"Anchor in THO" governance principle Joan Harper invokes: ValueSets
that are not `required`-bound to a `code` data type belong in THO so they
can be reused across IGs and lifecycled by the terminology infrastructure
rather than by an IG project. The disposition aligns with how the CARIN
WG has begun treating related artifacts (the Block-Vote-6 announcement
explicitly flagged "May move this ValueSet to THO for a future version
based on harmonization work with other IGs"), and it removes a known
source of confusion when other IGs (e.g., Da Vinci PDex) want to bind to
the same concept.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Keep the ValueSet's canonical URL inside the CARIN BB IG namespace for
STU2.x maintenance, but commit to the THO move on a planned
ballot-cycle boundary by:

1. Filing a UTG ticket immediately, but scoping it to **publish a
   parallel** `terminology.hl7.org`-anchored ValueSet (same id, codes,
   and intensional definition) without retiring the IG-anchored one.
2. Marking the IG-anchored ValueSet as `status = retired` /
   `experimental = false` only at the next major CARIN BB version, and
   adding a banner referring implementers to the THO canonical.
3. Updating `ExplanationOfBenefit` profile bindings to the THO canonical
   only at that next major version, so that already-conformant payers
   are not forced to re-validate mid-cycle.

#### Justification

This is what FM WG actually voted 13-0-0 for on 2022-07-26 ("Considered
for Future Use … may move … for a future version based on harmonization
work with other IGs") before Lynn Laakso reverted it. It honors the
governance ask without breaking deployed payer endpoints that have
indexed against the IG-anchored canonical, and it lines up the move with
the broader harmonization work the WG referenced (e.g.,
benefitpaymentstatus moving from `supportingInfo` to `adjudication` per
FHIR-37706/37726). The principal trade-off vs. Disposition A is a longer
window of duplicated artifacts; the offsetting benefit is a single,
well-signposted breaking change instead of an in-cycle URL churn.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Mark FHIR-35425 as **Not Persuasive**: keep
`ValueSet-C4BBPayerBenefitPaymentStatus` anchored under the CARIN BB IG
canonical and do not file a UTG move. Cite the same rationale the WG
applied to peer tickets in Block-Vote-6 (e.g., FHIR-35403, FHIR-35413,
FHIR-35414, FHIR-35415, FHIR-35417, FHIR-35432, FHIR-35434, FHIR-35446,
FHIR-35447): "ValueSet is not complex, fairly specific to this IG, and
does not warrant the maintenance of moving to THO."

#### Justification

`C4BBPayerBenefitPaymentStatus` is a small, IG-specific enumeration that
has no demonstrated reuse outside CARIN BB today, and its companion
CodeSystem (`C4BBPayerBenefitPaymentStatus` CS) is similarly IG-scoped.
Moving it to THO incurs ongoing UTG governance overhead, breaks existing
canonical references in deployed payer Bulk Data exports, and forces a
new IG version + implementer migration — for a ValueSet whose stewardship
the CARIN WG is best placed to maintain. The FM WG has consistently
applied this rationale to similar Block-Vote-6 ValueSets that were not
externally reused, and applying it here would produce a consistent
disposition pattern across the cluster.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

The original 13-0-0 vote captured the WG's actual consensus: the move to
THO is desirable but only meaningful once external harmonization
("other IGs" wanting to bind to the same concept) actually materializes,
and forcing it inside an STU2 maintenance cycle creates more implementer
churn than it relieves. Disposition A is the principled answer but is
hard to justify in isolation for one ValueSet when peer IGs aren't yet
asking for it; Disposition C closes the door on the harmonization the WG
itself flagged as plausible. Disposition B is the only option that both
(a) preserves the standing 13-0-0 intent that Lynn Laakso's revert did
not contradict — the revert was about timing post-STU2, not about
rejecting the move — and (b) protects already-deployed payer canonicals
while still committing the WG to file the UTG ticket now so the THO
ValueSet is ready when the next major CARIN BB cycle (or a sibling IG)
needs it. The WG should also batch-disposition this ticket with its
Block-Vote-6 ValueSet peers (FHIR-35424, FHIR-35426, FHIR-35427,
FHIR-35437) that received the same "may move … for a future version"
language so the cluster moves together.

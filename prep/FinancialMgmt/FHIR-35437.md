## Summary

This ticket — imported as Comment 49 by Ron G. Parker — asserts that the
defining (canonical) URL for the `CMSMS3MAPAPRDRG` ValueSet in the CARIN IG
for Blue Button (STU2) should be anchored at `terminology.hl7.org` (THO)
rather than under `hl7.org/fhir`. Per the broader pattern this ticket is part
of, only ValueSets whose required binding is to a `code` data type belong
under `hl7.fhir.org/...`; everything else should live in THO. The reporter
also asks that a companion UTG ticket be opened to register the ValueSet in
THO.

The ticket was originally bundled into **CARIN BB STU2 Block-Vote-6** (see
the Zulip announcement) and dispositioned "Considered for Future Use" on
2022-07-26 (vote 13-0-0, Spears/Conner) with the rationale that the WG may
move this ValueSet to THO in a future version pending harmonization work
with other IGs. That resolution was **reverted** on 2022-11-28 by lynn_laakso
("re-open post STU2 publication"), and the ticket is currently back in
**Triaged** status awaiting fresh disposition. No other Jira tickets are
cross-referenced, but the Zulip block-vote announcement enumerates ~25 sibling
tickets (FHIR-35390…FHIR-35447, plus FHIR-36486/36487, FHIR-37706/37726/37727/37728)
that follow the same THO-vs-hl7.org/fhir refactor pattern across CARIN BB
CodeSystems and ValueSets.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Move the canonical URL of `ValueSet-CMSMS3MAPAPRDRG` from
`http://hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/ValueSet/CMSMS3MAPAPRDRG` to
`http://terminology.hl7.org/ValueSet/CMSMS3MAPAPRDRG` in a future version of
the CARIN IG for Blue Button. File a companion UTG ticket to register the
ValueSet (and any underlying CodeSystem references it relies on) in THO,
deprecate the old canonical in CARIN BB with a `replacedBy`/retirement note,
and update every CARIN BB binding that points at the old URL.

#### Justification

This aligns with HL7's documented terminology curation policy that
non-`code`-bound ValueSets and the CodeSystems they draw from should live in
THO, not in IG-specific namespaces. It is consistent with how the WG is
handling almost every sibling ticket in Block-Vote-6 ("Plan on making change
when external CodeSystem 'all codes' ValueSet is published (UP-316)" or
"May move this ValueSet to THO for a future version") and gives the CARIN
content the same long-term governance, versioning, and reuse story as other
cross-IG terminology assets. The reopen comment ("re-open post STU2
publication") indicates the WG explicitly wants this revisited now that
STU2 ship pressure is gone.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Defer the canonical-URL move and instead group FHIR-35437 with its sibling
"CMS/3M MS-DRG"-style ticket(s) into a single coordinated UTG/THO migration
work item that addresses *all* CARIN BB ValueSets currently dispositioned
"Considered for Future Use" pending UP-316 (the external "all codes"
ValueSet publication). Mark FHIR-35437 "Persuasive with Modification" with a
resolution pointing at that umbrella tracker, and re-affirm the
"Considered for Future Use" disposition until the dependency is in place.
No code change in the current CARIN BB build cycle.

#### Justification

The `CMSMS3MAPAPRDRG` ValueSet enumerates an externally-curated CMS/3M code
list whose source-of-truth is not HL7. Moving the ValueSet's canonical URL
to THO without first having the underlying CodeSystem properly registered
and versioned in THO (the UP-316 dependency) risks publishing a broken or
ill-defined ValueSet in the central terminology server, and creates two
canonical URLs that implementers would have to follow during the transition.
Bundling the move with the broader THO migration avoids piecemeal
canonical-URL churn for implementers and keeps the change set defensible as
a single coordinated terminology-governance action. The original 13-0-0
"Considered for Future Use" vote already reflected this reasoning; the
reopen is best resolved by tying the ticket to the umbrella tracker rather
than acting unilaterally.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Resolve as **Not Persuasive**, leaving the canonical URL of
`ValueSet-CMSMS3MAPAPRDRG` under `hl7.org/fhir/us/carin-bb/...`. Note in
the resolution that the ValueSet is fairly specific to CARIN BB, that the
underlying code list is externally maintained by CMS/3M (so THO would not
become the source of truth in any meaningful sense), and that the
maintenance overhead of moving it does not warrant the disruption to
implementers who already consume the existing canonical.

#### Justification

This rationale is exactly the one the WG used to dispose of several
structurally identical sibling tickets in the same block vote (e.g.,
FHIR-35432, FHIR-35434, FHIR-35435, FHIR-35436, FHIR-35439, FHIR-35446,
FHIR-35447), all marked "Not Persuasive — ValueSet is not complex, fairly
specific to this IG, and does not warrant the maintenance of moving to
THO." If the WG concludes that `CMSMS3MAPAPRDRG` falls into the same
category — a relatively narrow, IG-specific assembly over an externally
curated code list — then declining is consistent precedent and avoids
canonical-URL churn for downstream payers and consumers.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

The original 13-0-0 vote landed on "Considered for Future Use" precisely
because the move depends on UP-316 / the external "all codes" ValueSet
publication and on broader cross-IG harmonization — none of which has
materially changed since the reopen. Disposition A is the right *direction*
but is premature in isolation: moving one ValueSet's canonical to THO ahead
of its CodeSystem and ahead of its sibling ValueSets creates exactly the
kind of fragmented, two-canonical state that implementers complain about.
Disposition C is too final and contradicts the WG's own stated intent to
revisit harmonization. Disposition B preserves the WG's prior judgment,
explicitly ties FHIR-35437 to the UP-316 / THO migration umbrella, and
gives implementers a single coordinated transition rather than a drip of
per-ValueSet URL changes. Workgroup should confirm whether the umbrella
tracker already exists or needs to be created as part of this resolution.

#### Topic: Section 3.3 measurement encoding, units, and nomenclature

These tickets cluster around Section 3.3 of the PHD IG, which governs how PHD measurements become FHIR `Observation`s. They cover GHS Observation Schedule mapping to `DeviceMetric.measurementPeriod`, the rationale for MDC float encoding (range vs. precision), whether UCUM emission should be a hard requirement, and what implementers should put in `CodeableConcept.text` when ISO/IEEE 11073-10101 has no agreed display. Together they refine how nomenclature, units, and human-readable text travel from a PHD into the IG's profiles.

## Summary

The ticket asks the Point of Care Devices (PoCD) FHIR IG to add a reference,
in its "Relationship to Other Projects & Guides" section, to the HL7 UDI
Domain Analysis Model (DAM) and to the UDI Pattern Implementation Guide,
and to keep that section in sync with future UDI-related IGs as they are
published. It is a documentation/relationship change rather than a change
to the Device resource itself; UDI data carriage in the model has already
been addressed by post-STU3 additions to `Device` (per jrhoads's 2018
comment).

The ticket has had a rocky history: it was originally voted *Not Persuasive*
in March 2018 (Reinhold/Faughn 9-0-0), then *Adjusted to Not Persuasive,
left as Deferred* in February 2019, and on 2026-04-01 was formally re-opened
on a motion by Erik Moll / Joe Quinn (13-0-1). The most recent substantive
comment, from `mcros` (2026-03-30), pushes back on the original ask —
arguing that the UDI DAM does not need to be called out under "Related
Projects" on the PoCD home page — and recommended re-opening so that the
disposition can be reconsidered.

Erik Moll flagged FHIR-15358 as a duplicate of
[FHIR-15359](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-15359), the sibling tracker
item from the same 2018-Jan PoCD batch. FHIR-15359 makes the same UDI
DAM / UDI Pattern IG referencing request but framed for "future documents"
and broader mapping pages (V2, DIM, SDC). Both tickets were re-opened on
the same April 2026 call and should likely be dispositioned together.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Update the Point of Care Devices (FHIR) IG home / introduction page —
specifically the "Relationship to Other Projects & Guides" section — to
add explicit narrative references to:

1. The HL7 **UDI Domain Analysis Model (UDI DAM)**, with a link to the
   published HL7 product page; and
2. The **UDI Pattern Implementation Guide**, with a link to its current
   canonical URL.

The change is purely narrative (no resource, profile, value set, or
constraint changes). Coordinate the same wording with FHIR-15359 so that
the "future documents" / mapping-page variant in that ticket reuses the
same canonical references. This matches the 2026-04-01 motion text
("Accepted - will add the requested content").

#### Justification

The PoCD IG already maintains a "Relationship to Other Projects & Guides"
section, and HL7's UDI DAM and the UDI Pattern IG are the two normative
HL7 artifacts that establish the conceptual model and identifier-pattern
guidance the PoCD IG implicitly relies on for `Device.udiCarrier` and
related elements (added post-STU3 per jrhoads's note). Calling them out
explicitly improves discoverability for implementers, is consistent with
how other Devices-WG IGs reference companion HL7 artifacts, and is
non-substantive (no conformance impact). The April 2026 13-0-1 motion to
re-open and accept indicates the workgroup now sees this as low-risk
documentation hygiene.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Rather than expanding the PoCD home-page "Related Projects" list, address
the underlying need by:

1. Leaving the PoCD home page narrative as-is; and
2. Adding the UDI DAM / UDI Pattern IG references on the **mapping pages**
   (V2 ↔ FHIR, ISO/IEEE 11073 DIM ↔ FHIR, SDC MDS Device profile mapping)
   where UDI semantics actually surface, plus a single sentence on the
   `Device` resource page that points readers to those mapping pages.

This aligns with `mcros`'s 2026-03-30 comment ("No need to refer to the
UDI DAM project under Related Project on home page") and with the
broadened scope `mcros` proposed on FHIR-15359 (cover DIM, V2, and SDC
mapping pages).

#### Justification

`mcros` argued that the home-page "Related Projects" list is the wrong
home for UDI DAM/UDI Pattern references — those artifacts are most
relevant where UDI data is actually mapped, not in the IG's general
relationship narrative. Putting the references on the mapping pages keeps
the home page focused on direct dependencies and surfaces the UDI guidance
exactly where an implementer working on a mapping is most likely to look
for it. It also makes FHIR-15358 and FHIR-15359 a single coordinated edit
(both tickets resolve to the same set of mapping-page changes) and avoids
the maintenance burden of keeping the home-page list in sync "in the
future" as new UDI-related IGs are published — a maintenance promise the
original 2018 wording explicitly asks for.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Resolve as **Not Persuasive** (restoring something close to the original
2018 disposition), on the grounds that (a) UDI carriage in FHIR is
already supported by `Device.udiCarrier` and friends added post-STU3,
(b) the UDI DAM is a conceptual analysis artifact that does not impose
conformance on PoCD, and (c) the request as written commits the IG to an
open-ended maintenance obligation ("and in the future any related IG
documents") that cannot be satisfied at publication time. Close without
changing the IG narrative; document this rationale on the ticket.

#### Justification

The 2018 vote (Reinhold/Faughn 9-0-0) reached this conclusion, and
nothing in the substantive content of the request has changed since
then — only the workgroup's appetite to revisit it. `mcros`'s 2026-03-30
comment is itself skeptical of the home-page change. Declining is
defensible because the model-level need (carrying UDI on `Device`) has
already been met, and a perpetual "reference any future UDI IGs" clause
is not implementable in a versioned spec. If the workgroup nonetheless
wants the references, they belong in a forward-looking artifact (the UDI
Pattern IG itself, or a UDI-companion page) rather than in PoCD's
relationship narrative.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

The April 2026 re-open vote (13-0-1) makes a pure decline politically
hard to land, so Disposition C is not the most likely path even though
it is defensible on the merits. Between A and B, `mcros`'s comment on
this ticket and the broadened scope they proposed on the duplicate
FHIR-15359 (mapping pages, SDC MDS Device profile) point clearly at B:
put the UDI DAM and UDI Pattern IG references where UDI semantics
actually live (the V2 / DIM / SDC mapping pages and a pointer from the
`Device` page), and leave the PoCD home page's "Related Projects" list
focused on direct dependencies. This satisfies the spirit of Terrie
Reed's original ask (UDI guidance is referenced and discoverable),
addresses `mcros`'s objection to a home-page entry, lets FHIR-15358 and
FHIR-15359 be dispositioned with one coherent edit, and avoids
committing the IG to an open-ended "future documents" maintenance
promise.

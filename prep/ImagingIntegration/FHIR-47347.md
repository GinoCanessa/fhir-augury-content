## Summary

FHIR-47347 reports that, in the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG,
the table / diagram listing the DICOM containers that make up a
TID 1500 Measurement Report is missing a row for the **Imaging
Measurement Group CONTAINER**. The reporter (Ana Kostadinovska) asks
that this container be added so the mapping documentation accurately
reflects the TID 1500 template structure. In discussion the structure
was further clarified against DICOM PS3.16 TID 1500: Numerical
Measurements, Qualitative Evaluations, and the Measurement Group sit
at the same level inside the Imaging Measurements Container, and
Derived Imaging Measurements are *not* nested inside a further
Measurements Group. The proposed resolution evolved through several
iterations of the container diagram and a replacement diagram drafted
by the reporter. The ticket has been marked **Persuasive with
Modification** and resolved 2025-10-17, with follow-up comments
confirming the diagram update and applying an equivalent fix to the
"DICOM SR Measurement Report – Mapping Overview" section of
`mapping.html`. One follow-up item remains noted in comments:
Qualitative Evaluations Container should contain `0..*` QE directly
with no intermediate container. No cross-referenced Jira tickets,
Zulip threads, or GitHub items were returned by FhirAugury for this
key.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Update the DICOM SR to FHIR Resource Mapping IG so the DICOM-container
table/diagram that enumerates the constituents of a TID 1500
Measurement Report includes the **Imaging Measurement Group CONTAINER**
as its own row, shown inside the Imaging Measurements Container and
alongside Numerical Measurements and Qualitative Evaluations. Apply
the equivalent row/diagram correction to the "DICOM SR Measurement
Report – Mapping Overview" section on `mapping.html`. Structurally:

- Imaging Measurements Container contains, at the same level:
  - Numerical Measurements (`0..*`)
  - Qualitative Evaluations Container → Qualitative Evaluation
    (`0..*`, directly, with no intermediate "Measurement Group"
    wrapper)
  - Measurement Group (`0..*`)
- Derived Imaging Measurements Container contains Derived Imaging
  Measurements directly; it does **not** wrap another Measurement
  Group layer.

This matches the resolution already recorded on the ticket
("Persuasive with Modification") and the uploaded
`tid_1500_measurement_report.png` diagram, and implements the
outstanding "Qualitative Evaluations Container contains `0..*` QE
directly" fix noted in the 2026-01-06 comment.

#### Justification

This is a faithful-to-TID 1500 correction. DICOM PS3.16 TID 1500 (the
normative source the IG is mapping from) places Numerical Measurement,
Qualitative Evaluation, and the Measurement Group at the same level
inside the Imaging Measurements Container, as Ana Kostadinovska
verified by reading the DICOM reference. The current table/diagram
omits the Measurement Group row and mis-nests Qualitative Evaluations,
which would mislead implementers trying to map DICOM SR instances to
FHIR `Observation` / `DiagnosticReport` structures. The change is
documentation-only, carries no conformance or schema impact, and the
reporter and assignee have already converged on the corrected diagram.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Apply the Accept-As-Requested correction, **and additionally** replace
the single "containers" table/diagram with a two-part presentation:
(1) a normative table that lists every DICOM container referenced by
this IG with its TID 1500 row-id, cardinality, and the FHIR resource
it maps to (e.g., `Observation`, `Observation` grouping, etc.), and
(2) the corrected structural diagram. Flag each container row with a
cross-link to the DICOM PS3.16 TID 1500 anchor on `dicom.nema.org`.
Introduce a small authoring convention in the IG that any future
container added to the mapping must appear as a row in that table
before it can be referenced elsewhere in the IG.

#### Justification

The original bug ("row is missing") suggests the table and diagram
have drifted out of sync because there is no single authoritative
listing of containers in the IG. A canonical, cross-linked table
prevents the same class of defect recurring (e.g., a future container
being added to a diagram without a matching row, or vice versa) and
gives implementers a direct jump from the FHIR mapping to the DICOM
source. The trade-off vs. Disposition A is scope: this is somewhat
more than the ticket literally asks for, but the extra authoring cost
is small and pays off every time a TID 1500 container set changes.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Decline the request on the grounds that the IG's container table and
diagram are illustrative, not normative, and that implementers should
rely directly on DICOM PS3.16 TID 1500 for the authoritative structure
of a Measurement Report. Close as "Not Persuasive" without modifying
the IG.

#### Justification

A defensible decline would rest on the argument that the IG's role is
to map DICOM SR constructs to FHIR resources, not to re-document
DICOM's own template hierarchy — and that any diagram inside the IG
is necessarily a simplification. However, this rationale is weak in
context: the missing row is not an illustrative simplification but a
factual omission that affects how implementers map TID 1500 to FHIR
`Observation`/`DiagnosticReport`, and both the reporter and the
assignee have already agreed the diagram was wrong and have corrected
it. This disposition is recorded for completeness only.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** A

The ticket reports a concrete factual error in the IG — the Imaging
Measurement Group CONTAINER is genuinely missing from the
container table/diagram, and the surrounding nesting of Numerical
Measurements, Qualitative Evaluations, and Derived Imaging Measurements
is inconsistent with DICOM PS3.16 TID 1500 as verified against the
DICOM source. The reporter and Jonathan Whitby have already converged
on a corrected diagram (Jira attachment 26546) and applied the parallel
fix to `mapping.html#dicom-sr-measurement-report-mapping-overview`;
the ticket is already resolved "Persuasive with Modification" and just
needs the outstanding "Qualitative Evaluations Container contains
`0..*` QE directly" tidy-up called out in the 2026-01-06 comment.
Disposition B is attractive as a follow-on authoring improvement but
should not hold up closing this ticket; it can be raised as a
separate Imaging Integration tracker if the work group agrees. There
is no substantive case for Disposition C.

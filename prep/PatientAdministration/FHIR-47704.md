## Summary

The ticket requests that the Interoperable Digital Identity and Patient
Matching (FHIR) IG include a worked example of a SMART Health Card (SHC)
that carries a Digital Identifier as an identifier, showing how that
identifier is represented inside the SHC payload. The reporter, Aaron
Nusstein, is asking for concrete, illustrative content (a sample card
plus accompanying narrative) rather than a structural change to the
specification itself. A Patient Administration commenter (juliemaas)
explicitly flagged the open question of whether such an example belongs
in an existing IG section or whether a new section needs to be added,
and offered a visual mock-up to seed the content.

The ticket was originally dispositioned by the Identity chairs as
"Considered for Future Use" (vote 8-0-0, 2025-06-17) on the basis that
it would be picked up in a future version after STU2. On 2025-12-10
Lynn Laakso reverted that resolution and re-opened the ticket post-STU2
publication, putting it back in front of Patient Administration for a
fresh disposition. There are **no cross-referenced Jira, Zulip, or
GitHub items** for this ticket in the FHIR Augury index, and a Zulip
search for both `FHIR-47704` and the phrase "Digital Identifier SMART
Health Card" returned no threads, so the disposition has to lean on
the ticket text and IG context alone. Keyword-similar tickets
(FHIR-33462, FHIR-33464, FHIR-53494, FHIR-48294, FHIR-48048, FHIR-48030)
are noted under *Related Jira Tickets* as orientation only — they are
not formally linked and do not change the scope of this request.

## Proposed Dispositions

### Disposition A: Accept As Requested

#### Proposal

Add a new worked example to the Interoperable Digital Identity and
Patient Matching IG that shows a SMART Health Card whose subject is
identified by a Digital Identifier. Concretely:

- Author a new example `Patient` instance whose `identifier` slice
  carries the Digital Identifier (`Identifier.type`, `system`, `value`,
  and any `assigner`/`period` consistent with the IG's
  Digital-Identifier profile), under the IG's example authoring root.
- Author a corresponding example SHC payload (a `Bundle` of type
  `collection` per the SHC profile) that includes that `Patient`, plus
  whatever supporting resources the SHC profile requires.
- Add a narrative page (or extend an existing SHC/SHL page) in the
  IG's pages root that walks the reader through the example: which
  field in the JWS payload represents the Digital Identifier, how a
  verifier would extract it, and how it differs from a non-digital
  identifier in the same slot.
- Cross-link the new example from the Digital Identifier page and from
  the SHC/SHL page so it is discoverable from both directions.

#### Justification

This is exactly what the ticket asks for, the change is purely
additive (new example + narrative — no normative content changes), and
juliemaas already supplied a mock-up that can seed the visual
narrative. Examples are a low-risk, high-value form of content for an
IG that is post-STU2: they do not change conformance, they reduce the
"how do I actually use this?" support burden on the WG, and they
directly answer a recurring implementer question (how a Digital
Identifier surfaces inside an SHC). It also aligns with the spirit of
the related ballot comments (FHIR-33462, FHIR-48294, FHIR-48048) that
have all been pushing the IG toward more concrete, less ambiguous
illustrations.

---

### Disposition B: Alternative Approach

#### Proposal

Roll FHIR-47704 into the broader SHC/SHL refresh tracked by
[FHIR-53494](https://jira.hl7.org/browse/FHIR-53494) and deliver the
Digital-Identifier SHC example as one item in a coordinated update,
rather than as a standalone change. Specifically:

- Mark FHIR-47704 as a child / dependency of FHIR-53494 in Jira.
- In the FHIR-53494 work, add the same example artefact described in
  Disposition A (Patient instance + SHC Bundle + narrative page), but
  also refresh the surrounding SHC/SHL content so the new example sits
  in a consistent, current section structure.
- At the same time, harmonise the narrative with whatever
  clarification FHIR-48294 ("Clarify how HL7 Identifier works") lands
  on, so the Identifier datatype is described consistently in the
  prose and in the example.

#### Justification

The example will land in SHC/SHL territory that is already known to
need a refresh (FHIR-53494) and uses the `Identifier` datatype that is
itself under clarification (FHIR-48294). Shipping FHIR-47704 in
isolation risks producing an example that has to be rewritten as soon
as the next set of changes land. Bundling avoids that churn, gives
implementers a single, coherent SHC story, and lets the WG amortise
the publication / regeneration cost across multiple tickets. The
trade-off versus Disposition A is timeline: implementers asking for
the example today have to wait for the bundled refresh. Given the
ticket already missed STU2, that delay is consistent with the chairs'
earlier "future version" framing.

---

### Disposition C: Decline

#### Proposal

Re-close the ticket as *Considered for Future Use* (or *Persuasive with
Mod → defer*), explicitly stating that the WG will not author a
bespoke Digital-Identifier SHC example and that implementers should
combine the existing SHC profile with the existing Digital Identifier
guidance themselves. Capture in the resolution that no normative gap
has been identified — only a missing illustration — and that the IG's
existing examples plus the SHC specification are sufficient to derive
the requested artefact.

#### Justification

This was, in essence, the original 8-0-0 disposition by the Identity
chairs in June 2025; the ticket was only re-opened post-STU2
publication and no new substantive information has been added since
then. Declining is defensible if the WG considers the missing example
a documentation nice-to-have rather than a conformance gap, and if WG
authoring bandwidth is constrained. The downside is that it leaves the
exact concern juliemaas raised ("is there an IG section where this is
mentioned or do we need to also add?") unanswered, and risks the same
ticket being re-opened again by a future implementer.

---

### Recommendation

**Recommended disposition:** B

Disposition B captures the actual implementer ask (a worked example —
Disposition A) while acknowledging the operational reality that
SHC/SHL content in this IG already has an open refresh (FHIR-53494)
and the `Identifier` datatype itself has an open clarification
(FHIR-48294). Shipping the example in isolation under Disposition A
would very likely require a second pass once those tickets land, and
the chairs' original "future version" framing already aligns with a
bundled delivery. Declining outright (Disposition C) is hard to
justify now that the ticket has been explicitly re-opened post-STU2 by
Lynn Laakso — the re-open signals that the WG does want to act, just
not necessarily on an STU2-era timeline. Disposition B gives the WG a
clear path: link FHIR-47704 to FHIR-53494, accept the example as
in-scope for that work, and use juliemaas's mock-up as the seed.

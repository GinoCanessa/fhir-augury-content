#### Topic: Quantity, unit conversion, and equivalence in FHIRPath

These three tickets all attack the same underspecified area of FHIRPath:
how `Quantity` values are added, compared for equivalence, and converted
between UCUM and calendar-duration units. They are explicitly being
treated as a single cluster by the work group — all three resolutions
defer to the umbrella ticket FHIR-56190 (Brian Postlethwaite's omnibus
rewrite of the FHIRPath quantity sections, staged on the
`BP-2026-03-quantity-preview` branch) and FHIR-53249's prep file
cross-links the other two as part of the same disposition.

##### Linked Ticket Group: FHIR-49525

FHIR-49525 frames the underlying question (what is the result unit of
arithmetic on `Quantity` values?) and Bryn Rhodes' proposed answer —
"convert both arguments to common UCUM base units" — is the same
mechanism the equivalence tickets adopt. FHIR-53249 (UCUM-axis
equivalence) and FHIR-53250 (calendar-duration-axis equivalence) build
directly on that disposition and are listed as in-cluster links from
FHIR-53249's prep file.

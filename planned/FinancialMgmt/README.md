#### Topic: claim-careteamrole CodeSystem and binding

Both tickets touch the `claim-careteamrole` ValueSet / CodeSystem family used by `Claim.careTeam.role` and `ExplanationOfBenefit.careTeam.role`. FHIR-31034 adds new role concepts (attending, referring, operating, …) to the underlying HL7/UTG CodeSystem; FHIR-50886 strengthens the binding on the consuming Claim and ExplanationOfBenefit elements from `preferred` to `extensible`. They share both the affected ValueSet and the FM care-team-role subject matter.

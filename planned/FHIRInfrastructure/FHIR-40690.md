## Feature Proposal

### Problem Statement

The FHIR Core `versions.html` page contains two narrative defects that the
FHIR Infrastructure work group classified as a **Technical Correction**
(highest priority):

- A spelling error: `fwe` instead of `few` in a sentence about which
  resources are intended to move to Normative status.
- A stale forward-looking sentence that reads "More information on plans
  for **Release 5** can be found on the HL7 product director's blog…"
  This sentence dates from before R5 publication; once R5 was published
  it should refer to **Release 6** (the next release in development).

These are pure prose corrections in an authored HTML page; there is no
schema, conformance, or terminology change.

### Proposed Change

Apply two narrowly-scoped narrative edits to the single FHIR Core page
`source/versions.html`:

**Change A — `fwe` → `few`** (in the sentence about which resources move
to Normative). The corrected sentence reads:

> HL7's general intent for the Release 6 is to move most of the resources
> in the Foundation, Base and Clinical layers (see the 'Category' tab in
> the Resources pages) to full Normative status, along with **a few**
> other resources including e.g., Questionnaire.

**Change B — "Release 5" → "Release 6"** (in the trailing paragraph
linking to the product director's blog). The corrected sentence reads:

> More information on plans for **Release 6** can be found on the HL7
> product director's blog. (Subscribing to this blog is a good way to
> keep current on significant events in FHIR development, including
> ballot and publication timelines).

No other edits are in scope (other technical corrections to the same page
have their own tickets — see Related Tickets below).

### Design Rationale

Both edits are unambiguous text-level corrections requested by the
reporter and agreed by the work group via a `Persuasive` resolution. No
alternative design is plausible for a typo / forward-reference fix of
this kind. The change:

- preserves all surrounding markup, anchors, and links;
- keeps the inline `<a href="https://onfhir.hl7.org/">…</a>` link target
  unchanged (only the surrounding prose is updated);
- aligns the page with the rest of `source/versions.html`, which already
  refers to Release 6 throughout (e.g., the Release-list bullet
  `<li>FHIR Release 6 (Normative) is version 6</li>` at line 206 and the
  introductory R6 paragraph at lines 793–797).

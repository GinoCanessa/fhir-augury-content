## Resolution Summary

> Will change this binding to be version-independent. (The code system and value set will move to THO.)

The approved modification (a) makes the binding on `ImplementationGuide.fhirVersion` (and its sibling `ImplementationGuide.definition.resource.fhirVersion`) version-independent, and (b) relocates the `FHIR-version` CodeSystem and its implicit ValueSet out of FHIR Core and into THO (the Terminology.HL7.org content managed by HL7 vocabulary in the `HL7/UTG` repository).

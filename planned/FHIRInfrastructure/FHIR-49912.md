### Resolution summary (for context)

The approved resolution is **not** the original "add a link to AD-Use"
request. Instead, it directs three actions:

1. Define a **new extension `Rank`** — `0..1 integer`, context
   `Address`, `Patient.contact`, `Organization.contact`. Definition:
   "Indicates the relative preference of an element within a collection
   of repeating elements. Lower numbers indicate a stronger degree of
   preference. Equal numbers indicate equal preference."
2. Update the **deprecation notes** of
   `StructureDefinition-iso21090-preferred` and
   `StructureDefinition-organization-preferredContact` to refer to the
   new `Rank` extension.
3. Submit a **separate** change request against
   `StructureDefinition-patient-contactPriority` suggesting it also be
   deprecated in favor of `Rank`. (Out of scope for this ticket's
   implementation.)

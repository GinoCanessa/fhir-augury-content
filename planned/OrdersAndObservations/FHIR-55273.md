## Resolution Summary

Resolution type: **Persuasive with Modification**.

Per the resolution description, the work group agreed to mark two ServiceRequest-scoped terminology artifact pairs as experimental (rather than migrating them to THO at this time):

- `servicerequest-status-reason` — change **CodeSystem** and **ValueSet** to experimental.
- `request-orderdetail-parameter-code` — change **CodeSystem** and **ValueSet** to experimental.

The modification relative to the ticket's original proposal is that the artifacts stay in FHIR Core (no THO migration); they are simply re-flagged as experimental so that future definition changes carry a lower stability guarantee.

## Feature Proposal

### Problem Statement

The ServiceRequest resource currently binds two element groups to locally defined terminologies that are candidates for migration to Terminology.hl7.org (THO): `ServiceRequest.statusReason` (bound to ValueSet `servicerequest-status-reason`, backed by CodeSystem `ServiceRequestStatusReasonCodes`) and `ServiceRequest.orderDetail.parameter.code` (bound to ValueSet `request-orderdetail-parameter-code`, backed by CodeSystem `RequestOrderDetailParameterCode`). These value sets are plausible candidates for cross-specification reuse (CDA, V2) and their definitions may evolve over the next few years. The ticket asks whether they should migrate to THO now.

### Proposed Change

Rather than migrate, mark all four artifacts (two CodeSystems + two ValueSets) as `experimental = true` in FHIR Core. This advertises their in-flux status to implementers without dislodging them from the Orders & Observations source tree.

Concrete changes (verified against clone `db79dcd`):

- `source/servicerequest/codesystem-servicerequest-status-reason.xml` — currently `<experimental value="false"/>` (line 23). Change to `<experimental value="true"/>`.
- `source/servicerequest/valueset-servicerequest-status-reason.xml` — currently `<experimental value="true"/>` (line 16). **Already correct**; verify only.
- `source/servicerequest/codesystem-request-orderdetail-parameter-code.xml` — currently `<experimental value="true"/>` (line 19). **Already correct**; verify only.
- `source/servicerequest/valueset-request-orderdetail-parameter-code.xml` — currently `<experimental value="false"/>` (line 19). Change to `<experimental value="true"/>`.

No element definitions, bindings, or cardinalities on ServiceRequest itself change. The ValueSet binding URLs on `ServiceRequest.statusReason` and `ServiceRequest.orderDetail.parameter.code` remain the same.

### Design Rationale

Marking artifacts experimental is the lightweight, low-risk option sanctioned by HL7 terminology governance when a value set is expected to evolve but is not (yet) being promoted to THO. It preserves all existing bindings and URLs, avoids the cross-repo THO migration overhead, and signals instability to downstream implementers. The resolution description explicitly states this modification; it is aligned with the Ready_to_Apply and R6-Release-note labels and the Orders & Observations work group's published disposition.

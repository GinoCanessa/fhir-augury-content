## Resolution Summary (captured for continuation)

From the ticket's Resolution Description (verbatim):

> The CompositionAttestationMode CodeSystem and ValueSet are owned by
> StructuredDocs (and represented on the Composition resource.) We would
> be supportive of moving them to THO.
>
> We will move the DocumentRelationshipType ValueSet and CodeSystem to THO.

Interpretation:

- **`CompositionAttestationMode` (CS + VS), bound at `DocumentReference.attester.mode`:**
  no action in this ticket. Ownership sits with **Structured Documents**
  (the CodeSystem/ValueSet are surfaced from `Composition`). O&O flagged
  support for a future move but did not approve the change here. Any
  DocumentReference-side work is out of scope.
- **`DocumentRelationshipType` (CS + VS), bound at `DocumentReference.relatesTo.code`:**
  **in scope.** Move both the CodeSystem and ValueSet from FHIR Core to
  THO (`HL7/UTG`). Update the binding on
  `DocumentReference.relatesTo.code` to target the THO-hosted
  ValueSet canonical URL, and retire the FHIR-Core-hosted copies
  (per the usual THO-migration recipe — authoritative copy in THO,
  FHIR Core references it).

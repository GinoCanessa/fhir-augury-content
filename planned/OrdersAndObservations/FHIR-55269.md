## Resolution Summary

The resolution (`Persuasive with Modification`) directs two distinct actions on the
THO-candidate terminology referenced by `Observation`:

1. **`observation-interpretation-context`** — change to **experimental** for both
   the CodeSystem and the ValueSet (no migration to THO).
2. **`observation-referencerange-normalvalue`** — **keep the ValueSet** in
   FHIR Core, but **move the CodeSystem to THO** (HL7 Terminology /
   `HL7/UTG`).

Verbatim resolution description:

> We will:
> - observation-interpretation-context:  change to experimental for CodeSystem and ValueSet
> - observation-referencerange-normalvalue:  keep ValueSet and move CodeSystem to THO

A clarifying comment from `jdlnolen` (2026-02-13) raises the question of whether
moving a CodeSystem to THO constitutes a breaking change. That question is
addressed in the Breaking Changes / Open Questions sections below.

## Feature Proposal

### Problem Statement

The original ticket flagged two CodeSystems referenced by `Observation` as
candidates for migration to the HL7 Terminology Authority (THO / `HL7/UTG`)
because (a) their definitions are likely to evolve in the coming years, and
(b) they may be useful outside FHIR (CDA, V2, other specs). The committee
agreed to a partial migration: only `observation-referencerange-normalvalue`
should leave FHIR Core; `observation-interpretation-context` is too narrow /
too FHIR-specific to migrate but should be re-flagged as experimental so that
implementers do not over-rely on it.

Affected artifacts in `HL7/fhir`:

- `source/observation/codesystem-observation-interpretation-context.xml`
- `source/observation/valueset-observation-interpretation-context.xml`

Change required by the resolution: ensure both artifacts carry
`<experimental value="true"/>`.

> have `<experimental value="true"/>` on lines 16 (CodeSystem) and 16

#### B. `observation-referencerange-normalvalue` (CodeSystem moves to THO, ValueSet stays)

The migration consists of three coordinated changes across two repositories.

**B.1 — Submit the CodeSystem to `HL7/UTG`** (THO).

The migrated CodeSystem must adopt a THO canonical URL. By convention for
THO-resident CodeSystems contributed from FHIR Core the canonical becomes:

- New canonical (proposed): `http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/observation-referencerange-normalvalue`
- Replaces FHIR-Core canonical: `http://hl7.org/fhir/observation-referencerange-normalvalue`
- Existing OID (preserve): `urn:oid:2.16.840.1.113883.4.642.4.2099`
- Concepts to carry over (verbatim from the FHIR Core CodeSystem):
  - `negative` — "Negative" — *The expected normal value for the observation is negative.*
  - `absent` — "Absent" — *The expected normal value for the observation is absent.*
- Other metadata to carry over: `name=ObservationReferenceRangeNormalValue`,
  `title=ObservationreferenceRangeNormalValue`, `status=active`,
  `experimental=false`, `caseSensitive=true`, `hierarchyMeaning=is-a`,
  `content=complete`, work-group `oo`, standards-status `informative`,
  fmm `5`.

Final URL/name decisions belong to UTG / THO leadership; the canonical
above is the THO convention but should be confirmed in the UTG submission
review.

**B.2 — Delete the CodeSystem from `HL7/fhir`** and update the ValueSet to
reference the THO CodeSystem.

- Delete: `source/observation/codesystem-observation-referencerange-normalvalue.xml`.
- Edit: `source/observation/valueset-observation-referencerange-normalvalue.xml`
  – change `<compose><include><system value="http://hl7.org/fhir/observation-referencerange-normalvalue"/>`
  to `<compose><include><system value="http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/observation-referencerange-normalvalue"/>`
  (line 31). All other ValueSet metadata (URL, OID, name, title, version,
  experimental, description, work-group, standards-status) is unchanged.
- Edit: `source/oids.ini` – remove the line
  `http://hl7.org/fhir/observation-referencerange-normalvalue = 2.16.840.1.113883.4.642.4.2099`
  (line 1368). The OID continues to live with the CodeSystem in THO. The
  ValueSet OID line at line 664 is **not** touched.

**B.3 — No change to `Observation.referenceRange.normalValue`**.

The element binding (`source/observation/structuredefinition-Observation.xml`
lines 1126–1142) points at the ValueSet
`http://hl7.org/fhir/ValueSet/observation-referencerange-normalvalue`,
which retains its canonical URL. Because the binding strength is `example`
and the ValueSet URL is unchanged, no edit is required on the
StructureDefinition.

### Design Rationale

- Marking `observation-interpretation-context` as experimental (rather than
  migrating it) is consistent with the FHIR pattern for narrow, illustrative
  example value sets that the WG does not want to govern long-term but
  cannot remove without breaking existing bindings.
- Moving `observation-referencerange-normalvalue` to THO matches the
  documented FHIR Core / UTG separation of concerns: codes that may be
  useful outside FHIR (CDA, V2) and whose meaning is stable across
  product families belong in THO so they have a single source of truth
  with their own governance lifecycle.
- Keeping the ValueSet in FHIR Core (rather than also moving it) lets the
  Observation StructureDefinition retain its existing binding URL —
  avoiding a binding URL break that would ripple to every implementer of
  R6 — while still placing the underlying codes under THO governance.
- Preserving the CodeSystem OID (`2.16.840.1.113883.4.642.4.2099`) on
  migration ensures that V2 / CDA artifacts that already cite the OID
  continue to resolve unambiguously after the move.

### Breaking Changes

- **`observation-interpretation-context`** — flipping the `experimental`
  flag from `false` to `true` is **non-breaking** in the strict
  conformance sense (no element, type, cardinality, binding URL or
  binding strength changes). It is a signaling change that may
  influence downstream IG authors.
- **`observation-referencerange-normalvalue` CodeSystem move to THO** —
  per `jdlnolen`'s comment, this requires explicit accounting:
  - The CodeSystem **canonical URL changes** from
    `http://hl7.org/fhir/observation-referencerange-normalvalue`
    to (proposed) `http://terminology.hl7.org/CodeSystem/observation-referencerange-normalvalue`.
    Any artifact that referenced the FHIR-Core canonical directly
    will break. **This is a breaking change** for any consumer that
    bound directly to the CodeSystem URL.
  - Mitigations that preserve compatibility:
    - The ValueSet canonical (`http://hl7.org/fhir/ValueSet/observation-referencerange-normalvalue`)
      is **unchanged**; consumers who bound to the ValueSet URL
      (the recommended pattern, and what `Observation.referenceRange.normalValue`
      uses) are not impacted.
    - The CodeSystem **OID** (`2.16.840.1.113883.4.642.4.2099`)
      and the existing `code` values (`negative`, `absent`) are
      preserved.
  - Net assessment: **breaking for direct CodeSystem-URL consumers,
    non-breaking for ValueSet consumers and OID-based consumers.**
    This must be called out in the R6 release note (label
    `R6-Release-note` is already on the ticket).

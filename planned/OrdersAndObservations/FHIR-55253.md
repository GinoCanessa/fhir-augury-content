## Resolution Summary (for context once the briefing is available)

The resolution applies five separate decisions, one per CodeSystem/ValueSet
pair currently bound from `Device`:

1. `device-availability-status` (CS `FHIRDeviceAvailabilityStatus`,
   bound on `Device.availabilityStatus`) — **move CodeSystem and ValueSet
   to THO (UTG)**.
   - Note from comment by JIRAUSER19407 (2026-03-30): the committer must
     also account for the disposition of FHIR-54324, which impacts
     `FHIRDeviceAvailabilityStatus`.
2. `device-nametype` (CS `DeviceNameType`, bound on `Device.name.type`)
   — **move CodeSystem and ValueSet to THO**.
3. `device-category` (CS `FHIRDeviceCategory`, bound on `Device.category`)
   — **move CodeSystem and ValueSet to THO**.
4. `device-specification-category` (CS `FHIRDeviceSpecializationCategory`,
   bound on `Device.conformsTo.category`) — **move CodeSystem and
   ValueSet to THO**.
5. `device-property-type` (CS `DevicePropertyType`, bound on
   `Device.property.type`) — **do not move**; this is genuinely an
   example. Mark both the CodeSystem and ValueSet with
   `experimental="true"` in HL7/fhir.

The first four require coordinated changes in **HL7/UTG** (add
CodeSystem + ValueSet at new THO canonical URLs) and **HL7/fhir**
(remove the local definitions and re-point the bindings on `Device.*`
to the new THO canonical URLs). The fifth is an HL7/fhir-only edit.

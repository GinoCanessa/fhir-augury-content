## Resolution Summary

Resolution: **Persuasive with Modification**.

The Boundaries and Relationships narrative for the `Observation` resource currently (in the original ballot text) implies that `Observation` is mainly used to record patient-generated information (e.g., self-test results), via the sentence:

> The Observation resource should be used to record patient-generated information (e.g., self-test results).

The approved resolution is to **delete that sentence** and **replace it** with a sentence that states the broader, neutral scope of `Observation`:

> The Observation resource is used to represent individual, atomic measurements or assertions, regardless of whether they are generated by a clinical service, a device, or by the patient.

The surrounding sentences in the paragraph (the contrast with `DiagnosticReport`, the list of laboratory/pathology/imaging reports belonging to `DiagnosticReport`, and the closing sentence noting that `Observation` is referenced by `DiagnosticReport` to provide atomic results) are kept verbatim. The only change is the substitution of one sentence for another inside the `Observation` "Boundaries and Relationships" prose. Implementation is tracked under PR [HL7/fhir#4024](https://github.com/HL7/fhir/pull/4024).

## Feature Proposal

### Problem Statement

The current `Observation` "Boundaries and Relationships" text contrasts `Observation` with `DiagnosticReport`, then asserts that `Observation` "should be used to record patient-generated information (e.g., self-test results)." Reporters (and Zulip / comment discussion on this ticket) flagged that this gives the misleading impression that `Observation` is *primarily* for patient self-reported / self-test results, when in fact `Observation` is the general atomic-result resource for the entire FHIR specification — used by clinical services, devices, and patients alike, and referenced by `DiagnosticReport` for grouped panels.

The wording also conflicts with existing FHIR usage where `Observation` records vital signs, laboratory results, device-emitted measurements, social-history assertions, and many other measurement-like data — none of which are inherently "patient-generated".

### Proposed Change

Edit the `Observation` boundaries/relationships narrative so that, in the paragraph beginning *"In contrast to the Observation resource, the DiagnosticReport resource typically includes additional clinical context …"*, the sentence

> The Observation resource should be used to record patient-generated information (e.g., self-test results).

is **removed** and **replaced in the same position** by

> The Observation resource is used to represent individual, atomic measurements or assertions, regardless of whether they are generated by a clinical service, a device, or by the patient.

The replacement sentence sits between the existing sentence ending *"…should be represented using the DiagnosticReport resource."* and the existing sentence beginning *"The Observation resource is referenced by the DiagnosticReport to provide the atomic results …"*. No other content in the paragraph or the surrounding section is altered.

Concretely, the resulting paragraph reads:

> In contrast to the Observation resource, the DiagnosticReport resource typically includes additional clinical context and some mix of atomic results, images, imaging reports, textual and coded interpretation, and formatted representations. Laboratory reports, pathology reports, and imaging reports should be represented using the DiagnosticReport resource. **The Observation resource is used to represent individual, atomic measurements or assertions, regardless of whether they are generated by a clinical service, a device, or by the patient.** The Observation resource is referenced by the DiagnosticReport to provide the atomic results for a particular investigation. …

This is a **prose-only change** in the authored HTML/XML narrative file for `Observation`'s introduction page in `HL7/fhir`. No StructureDefinition, ValueSet, CodeSystem, search parameter, or example needs to change.

### Design Rationale

- The replacement sentence preserves the original intent of contrasting atomic results against grouped reports while removing the inaccurate scoping to "patient-generated information".
- It explicitly enumerates the three common producers — *clinical service*, *device*, *patient* — which matches how `Observation` is used across the spec (vital signs, lab results, device data, patient-reported outcomes, self-tests).
- Comments on the ticket considered an alternative, more elaborate rewrite that would have introduced new SHOULD/MAY guidance about when self-performed/at-home tests warrant a `DiagnosticReport` for grouping vs. a standalone `Observation`. The work group rejected the broader rewrite for this ticket — that material is appropriately handled in the at-home testing IG and any future O&O grouping work, not in `Observation`'s base boundaries text. The "Persuasive with Modification" resolution therefore lands on the minimal, neutral one-sentence replacement.
- Keeping the surrounding prose verbatim avoids re-litigating the unrelated `ClinicalAssessment` / "summative variable" sentences and avoids unintended diff churn for downstream IGs that quote this section.

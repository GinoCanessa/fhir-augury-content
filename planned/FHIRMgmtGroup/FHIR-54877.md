## Resolution Summary

Resolution: **Persuasive** — "Will fix".

The reporter requested correction of a typo in the FHIR contributor credits
list: their company name is misspelled as "ComuGroup Medical" and should
be "CompuGroup Medical".

Exact change required:

> Reinhard Egelkraut (ComuGroup Medical) → Reinhard Egelkraut (CompuGroup Medical)

## Feature Proposal

### Problem Statement

The contributor credits list in the FHIR Core specification (rendered as
`credits.html` on the published site) contains a misspelling of one
contributor's affiliation. The contributor (Reinhard Egelkraut) is correctly
listed by name, but their employer is shown as "ComuGroup Medical" instead
of "CompuGroup Medical". This is a purely cosmetic / attribution defect with
no behavioural or conformance impact.

### Proposed Change

Update the single offending line in `source/credits.html` so that the
affiliation reads `CompuGroup Medical`:

- **File:** `source/credits.html`
- **Old:** `<li>Reinhard Egelkraut (ComuGroup Medical)</li>`
- **New:** `<li>Reinhard Egelkraut (CompuGroup Medical)</li>`

No other content, ordering, or markup is to change.

> Medical)</li>`. The fix appears to already be applied on `master`. The

### Design Rationale

This is a "Technical Correction" with resolution "Persuasive" / "Will fix".
The only acceptable change is the precise spelling correction requested by
the reporter; no design alternatives apply. The fix is one character

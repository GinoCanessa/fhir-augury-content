## 1  Summary

The fluoxetine 20 mg medication is correctly identified as a **capsule** (not a tablet) throughout the example text, but the dispensing quantity still reads "30 tablet" instead of "30 capsule." The ticket asks that every occurrence of "30 tablet" in the fluoxetine example fragments be changed to "30 capsule" to match the dosage-form description.

## 2  Resolution Details

> Auto-approved (Persuasive)

A "Persuasive" resolution requires implementation. The change is a straightforward text correction in example/narrative content only—no structural or data-type changes.

---

## 3  Feature Proposal

Replace the word **"tablet"** with **"capsule"** in the dispensing-quantity portion of every fluoxetine 20 mg example fragment so the full string reads:

```
fluoxetine 20 mg capsule 1 capsule oral 1 time daily now (30 capsule; 3 refills)
```

Other SSRI entries (citalopram, escitalopram, paroxetine, sertraline) correctly use "tablet" since those medications are dispensed as tablets. Only the fluoxetine lines are affected.

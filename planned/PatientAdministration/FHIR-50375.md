## Resolution Summary

The resolution (`Persuasive with Modification`) calls for two narrative-only
edits to the **PersonalRelationship** resource:

1. Rework the `.source` Comments to use the worked example below, making the
   direction of the relationship explicit:

   > For example, if the relationship is `daughter`, then the individual that
   > is the `.source` is the `daughter` of the `.target`.
   >
   > In a family with mother (Jane) and father (John), with a child (Mary)
   > there could be several personal relationship resources including:
   >
   > - Mary (source) is the daughter (relationship) of Jane (target)
   > - Jane (source) is the mother (relationship) of Mary (target)
   > - Mary (source) is the assumed (confidence) the daughter (relationship)
   >   of John (target)
   > - …

2. Update the `.target` Comments so it refers to the same example on
   `.source` (instead of duplicating it with the wrong directionality).

3. Make the formatting of element references (`.source`, `.target`,
   `daughter`, …) consistent across the two Comments blocks.

The resolution explicitly notes that ValueSet binding concerns are tracked in
other Jira issues and are **out of scope** for this ticket.

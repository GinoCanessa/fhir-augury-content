## Resolution Summary

The ticket was auto-approved as Persuasive. It identifies three typographical errors across three pages of the Women's Health Technologies (WHT) Coordinated Registry Network (CRN) FHIR Implementation Guide (published at `http://hl7.org/fhir/us/womens-health-registries/2019May/`). The corrections are:

1. **Profiles page** (`profiles.html`): "Faciltiy ID" → "Facility ID" (spelling correction in the CDE-to-FHIR mapping table).
2. **Index/Home page** (`index.html`): "The WHT CRN FHIR IG will also leverage the Structured Data Capture (SDC) FHIR IG." → "The WHT CRN FHIR IG will also leverage the Structured Data Capture (SDC), US Core, Patient Reported Outcomes (PRO) and DAF-Research FHIR IGs." (incomplete list of leveraged IGs).
3. **CRN Overview page** (`crn-overview.html`): "a patients' clinical data" → "a patient's clinical data" (incorrect possessive form — singular possessive required).

## Feature Proposal

### Problem Statement

The published WHT CRN IG contains three textual errors that cause confusion about which IGs are leveraged by the project and contain minor spelling/grammar issues that reduce the professional quality of the specification.

### Proposed Change

Correct three typos in the IG source files:

1. In the CDE-to-FHIR mapping HTML table, fix the misspelling "Faciltiy" to "Facility".
2. In the introduction section of the home page, expand the incomplete sentence to list all four leveraged IGs (SDC, US Core, PRO, and DAF-Research).
3. In the Background section of the CRN Overview page, change the plural possessive "patients'" to the singular possessive "patient's".

### Design Rationale

These are straightforward textual corrections with no structural, semantic, or normative impact. The ticket was auto-approved as Persuasive, indicating consensus that the changes are non-controversial and clearly correct.

## Implementation Plan

### Prerequisites

None. These are text-only corrections with no dependencies.

### Step-by-Step Tasks

#### HL7/coordinated-registry-network

1. **Fix "Faciltiy" typo in CDE mapping table**
   - File: `source/pages/_includes/CDEs_FHIR.html`
   - Action: Change `<td class="maptable16">Faciltiy ID</td>` to `<td class="maptable16">Facility ID</td>`
   - Details: This is in the last `<tr>` row of the mapping table (the row mapping the Facility ID CDE to `Location.identifier` / `us-core-location`).

2. **Expand list of leveraged IGs on the home page**
   - File: `source/pages/index.md`
   - Action: In the Introduction section, change:
     - From: `The WHT CRN FHIR IG will also leverage the Structured Data Capture (SDC) FHIR IG.`
     - To: `The WHT CRN FHIR IG will also leverage the Structured Data Capture (SDC), US Core, Patient Reported Outcomes (PRO) and DAF-Research FHIR IGs.`
   - Details: The current text only mentions SDC, but the IG actually leverages four IGs. The profiles page and CRN overview page already reference US Core, PRO, and DAF-Research; this brings the home page into alignment.

3. **Fix possessive form in CRN Overview**
   - File: `source/pages/crn-overview.md`
   - Action: In the Background section, change:
     - From: `Capturing a holistic picture of a patients' clinical data`
     - To: `Capturing a holistic picture of a patient's clinical data`
   - Details: The sentence refers to a single patient's clinical data in a registry context ("a patients'" is grammatically incorrect; the singular possessive "a patient's" is correct).

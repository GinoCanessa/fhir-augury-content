**Description:**
The reporter identified a typo in Section 3.3 ("Common Challenges When Transforming FHIR to OMOP") of the FHIR to OMOP IG. A section heading reads "Data Completeness, **Missiness** & Integrity" when it should read "Data Completeness, **Missingness** & Integrity" — the letter "g" was missing from "Missingness."

**Resolution Description:**
> We will be looking for a proper word.

The work group accepted the comment as persuasive and determined the correct term is "Missingness," a standard epidemiological term referring to the absence of data values that were intended to be collected.

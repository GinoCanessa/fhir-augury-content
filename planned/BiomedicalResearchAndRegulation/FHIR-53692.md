## Feature Proposal

Rename `ResearchStudy.primaryPurposeType` (0..1 CodeableConcept) to `ResearchStudy.purposeType` (0..* CodeableConcept) to support multi-value purpose classification. This change:

1. **Renames** the element from `primaryPurposeType` to `purposeType`
2. **Changes cardinality** from 0..1 to 0..*
3. **Creates a new expanded CodeSystem/ValueSet** (`research-study-purpose-type`) with 21 concepts (up from the existing 8) as a temporary example in the FHIR build
4. **Sets binding strength** to `example` (temporary, until BRR develops and publishes the target ValueSet in THO)

The change is motivated by use cases from the Evidence Based Medicine on FHIR IG, EU CTIS, and other projects that require multi-purpose classification for research studies.
